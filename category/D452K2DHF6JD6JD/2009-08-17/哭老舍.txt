<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 73.5pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 7.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"></SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT>&nbsp;</P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我每逢给学生讲授老舍的作品前，在翻阅查找有关老舍先生生平事迹的各种资料时，都要哭上几次的。尤其是看到那些具有权威性的什么“百科辞典”、“教学手册”、“大学语文”之类，更是如是。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">近来，中央电视台又播放有人改编了的《龙须沟》，快到</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">号了，我不禁又哭了。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">老舍，原名舒庆春，字舍予，北京人，满族。他是受人尊敬的教授、令人钦敬的爱国主义作家、让人羡佩的戏剧家、使人叹服的文学语言大师、叫人感佩的人民文学艺术家。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">老舍</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1913</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年入北京师范学校读书，</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1918</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年毕业后，先后在北京、天津任小学校长、中学教师等职。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1924</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年赴英国，在伦敦大学东方学院任教。在任教的百忙中，创作了长篇小说《老张的哲学》、《赵子曰》、《二马》。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1930</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年，他归国途经新加坡，在新加坡小居时，又写了中篇小说《小坡的生日》。回国后在济南齐鲁大学、青岛山东大学任教，并利用课余时间和节假日写了长篇小说《大明湖》、《猫城记》、《离婚》、《天牛赐传》，中篇小说《我这一辈子》，短篇小说集《赶集》、《樱海集》、《蛤藻集》等。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1936</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年创作了著名的长篇小说《骆驼祥子》。抗日战争暴发后，被名人学士推举为中华全国文艺界抗敌协会的总务部主任，为团结和组织文艺工作者参加抗日活动做了许多有益的工作。在此期间，又创作了大量的作品，如长篇小说《火葬》、《四世同堂》的一、二部（《惶惑》、《偷生》），短篇小说集《火车集》、《贫血集》、《东海巴山集》，话剧《张自忠》、《面子问题》，长诗《剑北篇》。为了适应抗战之需要，还积极从事鼓词、诗歌的创作。抗日战争胜利后，他应聘去美国讲学，在讲学期间，又完成了《四世同堂》的第三部《饥荒》，创作了长篇小说《鼓书艺人》。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年应祖国电召回到北京。中华人民共和国成立后，历任中国文联副主席、中国作协副主席、北京市文联主席等职，满怀激情的歌颂新中国和社会主义制度，一心一意跟着共产党走。从</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1950</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年到</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1956</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年创作了《方珍珠》、《龙须沟》、《春华秋实》、《西望长安》、《茶馆》、《红大院》等十多个剧本，《无名高地有了名》等数十篇报告文学、杂文、通俗文学，成为老一辈作家中解放后创作量最大的作家，荣获北京市“人民艺术家”和“劳动模范”称号……</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">就是这位孜孜不倦为社会主义增砖添瓦的人，就是这位呕心沥血为社会主义歌功颂德的人，就是这位诚心实意为社会主义埋头苦干的人，在</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1966</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">23</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日他和箫军、荀慧生等</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">30</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多名作家、艺术家，被挂上“牛鬼蛇神”、“反动文人”的牌子，押至北京孔庙大成门前，被迫向焚毁京剧服装、道具的大火下跪。老舍被打的血流满面、遍体鳞伤，后被押回市文联，又因“对抗红卫兵”，加挂“现行反革命”的牌子，遭到变本加厉的毒打，直至</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日凌晨。“红卫兵”勒令老舍</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日上午到市文联继续接受批斗。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman"><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </SPAN>1966</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，老舍没有去市文联接受批斗，他径直去了北京城西北角外的太平湖，思考一天，，毅然入夜投湖……</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">在那颠倒黑白、混淆是非的“文化大革命”中，到什么地方去说理？那真是叫天天不应，喊地地不灵的年代呀！一生光明磊落的老舍，只有以死抗争，这是对“文化大革命”最大最有力的抗争，除了一死，再没有比这更有力了！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">孰不知，这种死，在那疯狂的年代，还会给死者强加上“自绝于人民，自绝于党”的罪名！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">四十多年过去了，“文革”中的冤假错案似乎都平反昭雪了。其实不然。就以老舍为例，请看现行的大学语文课本以及十一届三中全会以后出的一些具有一定权威性的“辞典”、“手册”中对老舍的介绍吧：</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">全国高等教育自学考试指导委员会组编，徐中玉、钱谷融主编的本科《大学语文》（</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2001</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年第五次版）中说：“</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1966</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日被迫害致死。”</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">……</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">说被“林彪、‘四人帮’迫害致死”，是官话、套话、假话、骗人的话！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1966</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月份，“文革”初期，还没形成林彪反革命集团，也没有形成“四人帮”，林彪、“四人帮”这些人也负不起掀起“文化大革命”这个罪责的！“致死”？怎么致死的？大概是不敢、不愿、不忍心说出入夜投湖这个事实吧？</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">说“他不堪忍受所谓‘文化大革命’的迫害而愤然轻生”，更是对老舍的侮辱！怎么可以说是轻生？在那疯狂的年代，在全国都搞“一片红”的时候，在全国都搞“早请示，晚汇报”的时候，在全国大小会议都举着小红本祝“万寿无疆”、祝“身体健康”的时候，你心里也不平，也愤慨，你也可能被冲击、被批斗，面对这些不平事，你肯“轻生”吗？你敢“轻生”吗？老舍就肯，老舍就敢！我们可以理直气壮的说，老舍的入夜投湖是对“文化大革命”的最有力的反抗和斗争，这种死比泰山还重！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">说“</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1966</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日被迫害致死”，更是模糊不清，这种不负责任的说法会使下一代乃至永远的下一代不明历史真相。被谁迫害？怎么致死？应该清清楚楚明明白白的说出来。</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">还有的书中，干脆不提及老舍是怎么死的。是不敢、不愿、不忍心提这回事？还是不理直气壮？还是想抹去“文化大革命”的血腥味？！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我们要提，我们要光明正大的提，我们要理直气壮的提，老舍是为抗争疯狂的“文化大革命”而投湖自杀的！这一死，同他一生中孜孜不倦的著书立说同样光彩照人！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我哭老舍，一生那么大的功绩，最后又以死来抗争那颠倒黑白、混淆是非的“文化大革命”，那时却喊您“自绝于人民，自绝于党”！您牺牲的太多太多了！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我哭老舍，好容易拨乱反正，人们有了出头之日，一些冤假错案平了反。然而，一些人对您以死来抗争“文化大革命”的壮举、这个重于泰山的死，却推到林彪、“四人帮”身上！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我哭老舍，有人说你的死是“不堪忍受迫害致死”，这不但是歪曲，这简直是在骂您，骂您是不堪忍受肉体痛苦的“软骨头”！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">错了</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">!</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">他们全错了！您不是软骨头，您是铮铮铁汉，您以死抗争“文化大革命”，这是英雄的壮举，这是对黑暗实力最大最有力的反抗！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </FONT></SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我哭老舍的死，我更哭一些人对老舍的死存有不公正的看法和不公正的评价！痛哉！愚哉！</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
