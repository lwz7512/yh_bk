<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>从</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1986</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年春到</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1988</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年底，我在中顾委机关工作了近三年时间。所见所闻，至今记忆犹新。从</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1987</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年到</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1997</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年十年间，我协助荣高棠，对</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1982</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>－</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1987</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年间中顾委机关情况作过一个比较系统的回顾，并辅以文字记述，这也使我对中顾委这一时期的概况有所了解。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>由于中顾委属于“二线机构”，其性质和工作方式比较特殊，也不作公开宣传，所以一些情况难为外人所知。在此，也只能对一些人和事作一些片断的回忆。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">一</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1982</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月，党的十二大成立了中央顾问委员会，即中顾委。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>十二大结束当天，</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>13</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，中顾委举行了第一次全会。在这次全会上，邓小平当选为中顾委主任，并就中顾委的任务和工作方针发表重要讲话。在谈到中顾委日常工作时，他说：“原则规定都有了，现在的问题是怎样具体实施。要理出几条，其中也包括我们工作机构的设置。我建议不要建立什么大机构，要简化，几个人就够了。顾问委员会的日常工作，请薄一波同志主持，我想减轻一些负担。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>说到这里，薄一波插话说：“有你压阵就行，具体的事情我们干。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>薄一波和邓小平的合作渊源很深。早在</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>40</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年代，在晋冀鲁豫中央局时期，邓小平任书记，薄一波任副书记。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>50</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年代至文革前，邓小平任中共中央总书记、国务院副总理，负责中央日常工作；薄一波任副总理，主管工交财贸，一直在邓小平的直接领导下工作。很显然，由薄一波主持中顾委日常工作，虽然大家都赞成，主要还是邓小平的主张。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>当时，中顾委有四位副主任：薄一波、许世友、谭震林和李维汉。从年龄和健康状况看，薄稍显“年轻”，身体状况和精力也更好，相比之下，由他主持日常工作也更为适当。在之后三年中，其他三位副主任就先后过世了。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>虽然中顾委的建立经过长时间酝酿，但在成立伊始，工作方针、工作方式和日常事务并没有切实的具体方案。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>14</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，薄一波在中南海主持召开中顾委第一次常委会。会议决定，由薄一波、耿飚、程子华、王首道、刘澜涛和萧克等六人组成临时工作小组，负责研究制定中顾委的工作方针和工作方式，包括办事机构的设置。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>六人小组几经研究，拟定了《关于中央顾问委员会工作任务和工作方法的暂行规定》，经常委会通过后上报中央。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>30</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，中央批准了《暂行规定》并通报全党。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>这一阶段，中顾委的办公事务暂由中办秘书局代管。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月，中办发出通知，中顾委在中南海永福堂设立办事机构，但通知中对办事机构的名称没有明示。六人小组决定暂时命名为“中顾委联络组”，以便开展工作。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>最早到联络组工作的人员，有来自中办秘书局的李润民、籍海平和阎继荣，警卫局的牟信之和郑朝举。他们是开办中顾委机关的第一批工作人员。李润民是秘书局副局长，兼任联络组负责人。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">二</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1983</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，中组部部长陈野苹约见国家体委（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>国家体育运动委员会</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）顾问荣高棠，当面通知他，中央决定，任命他为中顾委秘书长。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>荣高棠感到很意外。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1982</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年底，他刚从国家体委副主任的位置上退居二线，担任体委顾问。至于中顾委，他只是知道是一个“新事物”，其他一无所知，也没有听到任何风声。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>他说：“我刚刚退居二线，已经七十一了，我干得了吗？”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>陈野苹说：“你不用说了，情况中央都知道。薄一波同志要为中顾委找一个秘书长，耀邦推荐了你，大家都赞成。你能干，书记处已经定了。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>荣说：“那我也得跟薄老谈谈，谈了再说吧！”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>陈说：“谈谈可以，这就算定了。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>荣高棠还是坚持说：“还是谈了再说吧！”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>第二天，国务院副总理万里召集国家体委领导班子前往中南海开会，宣布荣高棠的任命。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>万里说：“荣高棠要到中顾委当秘书长，为老头儿们服务。这个工作不好搞，是个挨骂的事儿。耀邦说，他（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>荣高棠</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）这个人，服务精神强，就要他去。书记处已经定了。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1983</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，薄一波在中南海办公室约见荣高棠。二人彼此熟悉，但在工作上从未打过交道。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>薄一波说：“中央已经决定，叫你当秘书长。成立了顾委，就要做工作。我找郑天翔（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>时为第七机械工业部顾问，中顾委委员</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>），他不干。耀邦推荐了你，大家都赞成。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>薄又说：“过去，在晋冀鲁豫，小平同志是书记，我是副书记。有事开个会，他说个‘干’，就不管了，剩下的事就让我去办。后来，小平同志又让我去管工交财贸，他是总书记，也是开个会，事情就让我去办。这次还是，他是主任，开个会，事情就让我管，他很放手。可我又忙不过来，所以，就找你来帮忙。过去在贺老总（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>贺龙</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）手下，体委的事都是你搞起来的，因此，大家都赞成你来当这个秘书长。具体怎么搞，咱们摸索着干。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，荣高棠到中顾委机关上班。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日，中央下达通知，公布他的任命。此时，中顾委机关名称正式确定为中顾委办公室。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>按照邓小平的意思，中顾委机关“不搞什么大机构，几个人就够了”，所以，机关组建的要领就是机构小，名称小，人员少。办公室实行秘书长负责制。秘书长下设办公室，下辖值班联络、文件机要和行政财务三个处，称一、二、三处。干部编制约</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>人左右，加上服务员、司机在内，全员在</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>25</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>人左右。这种情况一直持续到十三大。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1984</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年秋，中顾委办公室迁至中南海“</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>301</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>”，薄一波的办公室也搬了过来。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">三</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>中顾委的老人，都是“打天下”的元老，都曾位高权重，个个资历深厚，身份不凡。尽管退居二线，但说话表态，分量都不轻。早在酝酿成立中顾委时，邓小平就说“我来当这个头”，当然也只有他才能“压得住阵”。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>作为邓小平授权的重要助手，薄一波的政治韬略和处世之道，与邓小平可谓相得益彰。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>面对中顾委的一班老人，他在把握大局、规范中顾委的作为和维护党的纪律方面表现的强硬姿态，很像邓小平；而在具体工作中的审时度势和缜密细致，与邓小平那种大刀阔斧、说一不二的风格又有不同，对邓小平主张的贯彻落实，是不可或缺的补充和配合。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>邓小平对于中顾委，的确就像薄一波所说，说个“干”，就不管了。在中顾委的前五年中（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>1987</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>年十三大，改由陈云任主任</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>），他只是在中顾委两次全会上讲过两次话，平时根本不在中顾委露面，对日常工作更是从不说话。事实上，薄一波就是中顾委日常事务的实际领导人。各方面情况和各项工作，是否报告中央，报告邓小平，又该如何报告，都是薄一波定夺，凡须报告中央，都由薄一波出面。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>当时，薄一波除了主持中顾委工作，还兼任中央整党指导委员会常务副主任、中央人事领导小组负责人、中央经济体制改革领导小组负责人等重要职务，头绪很多，身处诸多政治要冲，责任重大。他的状态，的确可喻为“日理万机”。在机关的几年中，经常看到他成天待在办公室，早来晚走，星期天也不休息。每天傍晚，常常是机关的人都走光了，他还在工作，直到路灯点亮的时候，他才离开。在机关通往他家的小胡同里，时常看到昏黄的灯光下，一个弯腰驼背的孤独身影，在秘书或卫士的陪伴下蹒跚而行。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>荣高棠对薄一波非常敬重，事无巨细，几乎天天请示汇报。尤其是干部调配，都要征得薄的同意。对于机关日常工作，荣抓得细，薄管得也细，两人都是亲力亲为，身体力行。荣既要配合薄做委员们的工作，完成中央或常委交办的任务，也要处理机关各项日常事务，工作强度相当大，经常也是一进办公室就不出来，直到下班。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>平时，大家和薄一波经常碰面，除了机要处的同志，大家和他直接接触并不多。每当向他问好，他只是笑着点点头，算是回应。我和薄一波直接接触只有一次。一天傍晚，我正要下班，薄的秘书过来找我，说是薄老有份文件想叫我帮着抄写誊清。我跟随到薄的办公室，薄站起来，笑眯眯地和我握手，操着山西腔连说两句：“抓你个苦工，抓你个苦工！”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>每当机关开会，荣高棠都把薄一波请来，和大家见见面，讲讲话。这时，薄一波从不批评大家，都是表扬和鼓励。大家不止一次地听到他总爱说的两句话：“你们年轻人，不要当大官，要做大事。”“我们这些老家伙，正一个跟着一个，往八宝山走哩！有的过了公主坟了，有的过了五棵松了！”</FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>中顾委成立后，面临的一个重要课题就是如何对待老同志，如何贯彻落实邓小平、胡耀邦和中央提出的“政治待遇不变、生活待遇从优”的政策。中央书记处强调，这是关系到政治上的安定团结，关系到党和国家长治久安的问题，应当成为我们党和国家的坚定不移的政策原则之一。这是薄一波和中顾委机关始终密切关注、审慎对待的问题。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>由于长期以来党内奉行干部等级制度的缘故，在党内生活中滋生了一种趋炎附势、人前人后的陋习，在对待老同志的态度上屡屡出现“人走茶凉”的问题。一些老人退下来后，对这类问题也比较敏感，时有不满或抱怨。</FONT></SPAN></P>
