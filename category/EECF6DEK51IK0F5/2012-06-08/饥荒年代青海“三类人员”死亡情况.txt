<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal align=left><BR><FONT size=2 face=黑体>1958 年6 月8 日，《人民日报》开始登载粮食亩产放“卫星”的浮夸报道。截至9 月25 日资料，小麦亩产最高数是青海柴达木盆地赛什克农场第一生产队的8586 斤，稻谷亩产最高数为广西环江县红旗人民公社130435 斤。</FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><BR><BR>　　1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年夏、秋，《人民日报》连篇累牍报道全国各地农业生产大丰收、产量放“卫星”的新闻。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，《人民日报》报道河南省西平县和平农业社小麦平均亩产</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">7320</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">斤的最新、最高纪录。因</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月以后小麦已经过了收获季节，看来西平县小麦高产“卫星”之最已成定局。出乎人们意料之外的是，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">22</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，还是《人民日报》，报道青海省柴达木盆地赛什克农场第一生产队小麦亩产</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8585</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">斤</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">两。西平县小麦高产“卫星”被打破了。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">柴达木盆地平均海拔约</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">米，属于高原气候，春天播种，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月收割。在这个时候放的这颗“卫星”，别的地方要赶超已经不可能了，因为已经过了小麦收获季节，要赶超只能等来年。在全国各地农业生产放“卫星”的“凯歌”声中迎来</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1959</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年。春节刚过，饥荒就开始蔓延，越来越严重。到了夏、秋收粮食收获季节，没有什么地方放高产“卫星”了。所以，赛什克农场</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年放的这颗小麦高产“卫星”，是全国最高的，全世界最高的。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">笔者在青海省公安厅工作</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年，到过全省所有的劳改农场。赛什克农场就是省公安厅劳改局的一个直属农场。在柴达木盆地的诸多劳改农场中，它的生产条件并不好，好的年景小麦亩产量最高也就是两三百斤。那么，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多斤的亩产量是怎么来的呢？</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1962</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，笔者随领导到赛什克农场检查工作，询问该场生产科科长（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312" lang=EN-US>1958</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">年放“卫星”时他是副科长，对放“卫星”一事很了解</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），了解</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年小麦亩产放“卫星”情况，下面是对话记录：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">问</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">：<SPAN lang=EN-US>1958</SPAN>年第一生产队小麦亩产<SPAN lang=EN-US>8585</SPAN>斤<SPAN lang=EN-US>6</SPAN>两是怎么来的？<SPAN lang=EN-US><?xml:namespace prefix = o ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" /><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">答</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">：领导吹的呗！<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">问</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">：怎么能上《人民日报》？他们的记者来过吗？<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">答</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">：《人民日报》有个记者住在西宁，我们场长给他打个电话，发了个电报，没想到报纸就登上了。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">问</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">：亩产才<SPAN lang=EN-US>200</SPAN>多斤。你们报了<SPAN lang=EN-US>8000</SPAN>多斤，太离谱了，不感到心虚吗？<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>我无言以对。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">下面让我们来仔细考察一下青海省劳改单位“大跃进”、粮食放“卫星”情况。这些情况，是王昭</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1961</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年到青海主政、纠正原青海省委在“大跃进”运动中所犯的严重错误中揭发出来、并被事实证明是确凿无误的。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1960</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年，公安部从内地将</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多万已判刑的人犯（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">很多人是因对“大跃进”不满而被判刑劳改的</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）调入地处高原、干旱少雨、气候恶劣的青海省。为了响应工、农业生产“大跃进”的号召，青海省劳改系统积极开展“大跃进”运动。青海省是个长期缺粮的省份，每年需要从其他省调入大量粮食，这么多犯人一下子调进来，吃饭成了大问题。于是开荒种地就成了“大跃进”的切入口。省里领导人不问青红皂白，不管有水、无水，不管气候异常能种不能种，不管种了是否有收获，也不管“三类人员”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">劳改犯人、刑满就业人员、劳教人员——下同</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）身体吃得消吃不消，大规模地开荒种地。提出“以开荒为纲，边开荒边播种，开一亩种一亩”，“不吃也得种；不打粮食也得种；只要能收些草，就得种”，“不种，就是右倾机会主义”。在这些完全失去了理智的错误口号指导下，在全省范围内，大规模地垦荒种地。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1957</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年，省劳改局直属劳改农场全部耕地</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万亩，到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1960</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年达到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">211</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万亩，增加了近</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">倍。但是，开荒越多，死人越多，经济损失越大，开垦出来的耕地很多不能种庄稼，把草原变成了荒漠，生态环境遭到严重破坏。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年播种面积由</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1957</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万亩，开荒扩大到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">430751</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">亩，粮食总产量为</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">130948304</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">斤。单位面积产量为</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">304</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">斤；</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1959</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年播种面积为</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">820997</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">亩，粮食总产量为</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">209353235</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">斤，单位面积产量为</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">255</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">斤；</SPAN></FONT></P>
