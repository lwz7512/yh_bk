<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">八路军与第二战区指挥关系的确定</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">八路军改编以后，按照</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1937</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; <P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，国民政府军事委员会明令，八路军改为第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">18</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">集团军，朱、彭为集团军总、副司令，编入第二战区战斗序列。朱德、彭德怀于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">14</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日通令全军。按照常规，八路军此时起即归战区司令长官阎锡山指挥。但八路军是中国共产党领导下的队伍，并明确声称执行“独立自主的山地游击战”，出现了新的指挥关系问题。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，八路军驻太原办事处主任彭雪枫赴位于雁门关南的太和岭口行营与阎锡山会晤，商谈了指挥关系问题。关于今后指挥八路军问题，阎锡山明确提出拟用两个原则：“一为事先相互商议；二为临时将计划部署通知八路军，由八路军自行酌量行动。”这种指挥关系的确立，为以后双方在处理作战方面的问题，明确了基本原则。当然，八路军的计划和战斗部署也会发生一定的影响。</SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">14</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，林彪率</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">343</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">旅、独立团和骑兵营到达平型关西南侧的大营镇待机。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">当日，林彪向中央军委和八路军总部发出在当前情况下</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师行动计划的电报。他在电报中说：“在广灵失守，灵丘附近到敌之情况下，原定</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师经灵丘到涞源的计划已不执行。现拟将</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">343</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">旅及师直集中大营，准备待敌侧攻大营东之平型关友军阵地时，我相机袭击敌之左侧后，歼敌一部，以扩大战果。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">毛泽东收到林彪的电报后，于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日致电林彪，指出：“日寇某军自天镇向广灵、关东军自蔚县急进。晋阎拟集中</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">14</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个团与敌决战，希望我</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师参加作战。我军应坚持既定方针，用游击战斗配合友军作战。此方针在京与蒋、何决定。周、彭又在晋与阎当面决定。基本不要动摇此方针。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">17</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，林彪致电毛泽东，提出他对当前</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师行动原则。他说，“我目前采取的行动原则，是在敌目前正在进攻的情况下，我先头旅以作战灭敌为主要任务。关于第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师目前行动方针，不只</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">343</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">旅应在现地域协同友军作战，师直及</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">344</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">旅亦应同样任务而靠近</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">343</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">旅，暂不应以做群众工作为中心任务而进驻阜平。理由是：（一）目前敌正在前进中，运动中，作战中，为我进行运动战之良好机会。我友军目前尚有抗敌人之相当力量，为能得到友军作战之良好机会。现地域为山地，为求山地战之良好机会。倘过此时机，敌已击破友军，通过山地并进占诸主要城市时，即较难求运动战、山地战及友军配合作战。（二）目前军民正在看我军直接参战，如我参战兵力过少，则有失众望。（三）兵力过小则不能将以绝对优势兵力消灭敌之一部以上。（四）目前须以打胜仗、捉俘虏，提高军民抗战信心，提高党与红军威信。（五）目前第一仗实以集中约一师的兵力为好，待尔后客观情况上已失去一师兵力作运动战之可能时，再分散作群众工作和游击。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">就在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">17</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日这天，毛泽东《关于敌情判断及八路军之战略部署致朱、彭等前方将领电》，请各将领提出意见并电复。林彪复电完全同意。可惜林彪复电的内容笔者没有看到，不过从</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日毛泽东致彭德怀电中对于林复电的内容有所披露。电文中说：“林彪同志来电完全同意我十七日的判断和部署，他只想以陈旅集中相机给敌以打击，暂时不分散。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">“陈旅”即指陈光为旅长的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">343</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">旅。该旅只有两个团，这与林集中全师兵力打仗的主张不够一致。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>林彪集中兵力打运动战的主张显然与毛泽东的思想相悖。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">毛泽东强调“独立自主的山地游击战”</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>毛泽东的战略思想与八路军的作战原则，是洛川会议通过的“独立自主的山地游击战”。为了贯彻落实这一方针，他对党内外、军内外，从各方面展开了一系列的说服工作。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">对国民党当局和地方实力派表明八路军的作战原则。早在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1937</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，毛泽东致周恩来、博古、林伯渠电指出：“在整个战略方针下，执行独立自主的分散作战的游击战争，而不是阵地战，也不是集中作战……”</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，毛泽东致彭雪枫电。指示彭“同各方接洽，在积极推动抗战的总方针下，要有谦逊的态度，不可自夸红军的长处，不可说红军抗日一定打胜仗……不可隐瞒红军若干不应该隐瞒的缺点，例如只会打游击战，不会打阵地战。”</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，致电彭德怀：“在晋在冀在京，均着重解释我军独立自主的山地游击战这个基本原则，取得他们的彻底了解与同意。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">告诫前方各将领，必须认识八路军在当前抗战中的地位和作用。他在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">17</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日致朱德等前方将领说：“红军此时是支队性质，不起决战的决定作用。但如部署得当，能起在华北（主要在山西）支持游击战争的决定作用。”在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日致彭德怀的电报中，再次强调：“今日红军在决战问题上，不起任何决定作用，而有一种自己的拿手好戏，这种拿手好戏中一定能起决定作用，这就是真正独立自主的山地游击战（不是运动战）。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">必须坚持“独立自主的山地游击战”的既定方针。他说：“为真正进行独立自主的山地游击战，为广泛发动群众，组织义勇军，创造游击根据地，支持华北游击战争，并为扩大红军本身起见。”对八路军的部署作了重新的规定。他电示第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师：“我应坚持既定之作战方针，用游击战争配合友军作战，不应动摇这个方针。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>强调分散做群众工作。“集中打仗则不能做群众工作，做群众工作则不能集中打仗，二者不能并举。然而，只有分散做群众工作，才是决定地制胜敌人，援助友军的唯一无二的办法。集中打仗在目前是毫无结果之可言的。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>教导前方将领：“不管决战胜败如何，太原与整个华北都是危如累卵。个别同志对于这种客观的必然的趋势，似乎还没有深刻认识，被暂时情况所诱惑。如果这种观点不变，势必红军也同阎锡山相似，陷入被动的，应付的，挨打的，被敌各个击破的境遇中。”他教导大家：“目前情况与过去国内战争根本不同，不能回想过去的味道，还要在目前照样再做。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">25</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，毛泽东致电周恩来并转刘少奇、杨尚昆、朱瑞，并告朱德、彭德怀、任弼时：“整个华北工作，应以游击战争为唯一方向，一切工作，例如兵运、统一战线等，应环绕于游击战争。华北正规战如失败，我们不负责任；但游击战争如失败，我们须负严重的责任。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">以上笔者引用了毛泽东</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1937</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">25</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日前有关我军的战略方针与八路军的作战原则的电报。从这些电报的字里行间，都表现了他反对集中兵力打运动战而强调打游击战的思想。</SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，毛泽东关于调整八路军各师活动地区致朱德、任弼时电：“（甲）日寇分两路进攻广灵、灵丘，晋军已放弃大同，绥远全境实际已失。以五台为中心之晋东北，日寇将以重兵进据，并继续进攻取太原。在此情况下，我三个师已无集中晋东北一处之可能，更无此必要。（乙）拟以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师位于晋东北，以五台为活动重心，暂时在灵丘、涞源，不利时逐渐南移，改以太行山脉为活动区。以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">120</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师位于晋西北，以管涔山脉及吕梁山脉之北部为活动地区。以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">129</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师位于晋南，以太岳山脉为活动地区。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">17</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，毛泽东关于敌情判断及战略部署致朱德等电，其中除敌情判断外指出：“过去决定八路军全部在恒山山脉创造游击根据地的计划，在上述敌我情况下，已根本不适用了。”“为战略上展开于机动地位，即展开于敌之翼侧，钳制敌之进攻太原与继续南下，援助晋绥军使之不过于损失力量，为真正进行独立自主的山地游击战，为广泛发动群众，组织义勇军，创造游击根据地，支持华北游击战争，并为扩大八路军本身起见，拟变更原定部署，采取如下之战略部署：（一）我</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">120</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师应集结于太原以北之忻县待命，准备在取得阎之同意下，转至晋西北管涔山等地区活动。（二）我</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">129</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师于适当时机，进至吕梁山脉活动。（三）我</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师则以自觉的被动姿势，现时进入恒山山脉南段活动，如敌南进，而友军又未能将其击退，则准备依情况逐渐南移，展开于晋东南之太行、太岳两山脉中。（四）总部进至太原地区，依情况决定适当位置。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">18</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，毛泽东致朱德、任弼时电：“恒山山脉是第一战区与第二战区接合部。敌主力必从此进。我林师现已当了正面，处于完全的被动地位。贺师决不能再用此方，应速向晋西北转进。”“兄应速去太原转雁门关与阎商贺师防地。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">当日，彭德怀关于华北战局及军事部署致毛泽东电：“目前部署：甲、以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">115</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师进到灵丘及涞源以南，相机侧击敌之一部。乙、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">120</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师及总直属队进到阜平、五台及灵丘以南，加紧发动群众及组织游击队。丙、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">129</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">师在南京承认宣言及边区政府发表，即出动晋西北，小部向绥远发展游击战争。”“上项布置请立即考虑电复，以便明午后往代县见阎，具体商定繁、五、盂、寿、平五县部署及晋西北、绥远部署。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
