<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">1933</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">年毛泽东在江西<BR><BR></P><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri><IMG style="HEIGHT: 286px; WIDTH: 524px" border=0 alt="" src="/upFiles/czhang2014tubian/201411111531188792.jpg" width=2362 height=1329>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 21pt"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体"><FONT size=3>王家祠堂当时是江西省行委（省委）与省苏维埃驻地，也是富田事变发生地，堂前广场即富田广场。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体"><o:p><FONT size=3>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体"><FONT size=3>富田事变王家祠堂</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1932</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，红一方面军总政治部秘书长兼肃反委员会主任李韶九奉总前委书记毛泽东之命，从总前委驻地宁都黄陂出发，带一连士兵前往吉安富田抓</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日他们到达江西省行动委员会（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">即当时的江西省委，下称省行委</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）所在地富田，李指挥士兵迅速包围省行委和省苏维埃办公地王家祠堂，然后冲进祠堂，将正在开会的几位负责人捆绑，并满屋搜查。被捕的人中有省行委常委、赣西南特委书记段良弼，省行委秘书长李白芳，省行委军事部部长金万邦，省苏维埃财政部部长周冕，红二十军政治部主任谢汉倡……连同一般工作人员共</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>120</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">多人。李韶九亲自审讯这些被捕的人，不许对方辩解，只许承认自己是</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团，然后交代其他</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团成员的姓名。否则便施以“地雷公烧香火”、“点天灯”、烧阴户等酷刑。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">由于严刑，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日凌晨，红二十军政治部主任谢汉倡被迫供出红二十军秘书长兼一七四团政委刘敌也是</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团。李韶九决定带一排人</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日赶赴</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>40</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">里外红二十军驻地东固，捉拿刘敌及其他</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团分子。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日早饭后正要动身，遇蒋军飞机来轰炸，“为防犯人逃跑”，李韶九便将一批“不重要的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团分子”</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>40</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">多人杀了，再押解谢汉倡前往东固。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">一七四团政委刘敌率领独立营正在前线，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日接到军部要他立即返回的急信，还以为是打了胜仗要他去领取慰劳品和补充兵源。想不到一进军部，就被当作</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团要犯控制，李韶九亲自审讯他。刘与李是湖南同乡，且早相识，知道硬顶不是办法，于是改用长沙话与李攀谈，附和李韶九与军长刘铁超（</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">3</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">人都是湖南人</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">），渐渐取得李的信任。李不仅不再说他是</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>AB</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">团，还要他好好干，示意将会倚重他，然后派人连夜送他回营。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">侥幸脱险的刘敌回到驻地，独立营营长张兴和政委梁学贻见他平安归来，非常高兴。刘敌将这天的遭遇说了一遍，二人愤愤不平。第二天（</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日）早饭后，刘召集张、梁二人商议，认定这次李韶九来，是总前委的阴谋，是企图消灭江西党与部队的干部。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">人商定请李韶九来讲话，趁机将他扣留。但血气方刚的张兴不等李韶九来便去军部质问，无异于自投罗网。刘敌闻张兴被扣，立即与梁学贻集合部队，迅速包围军部，释放了被捕的谢汉倡和张兴，抓了与李韶九合作的军长刘铁超，但跑了李韶九。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">由于担心李韶九逃回富田杀害被捕的同志，刘敌率机枪连与独立营马不停蹄直奔富田，黄昏时赶到富田，包围省行委驻地王家祠堂，缴了李韶九带来的士兵的枪，释放了段良弼等被捕的同志。支持李韶九的曾山、古柏等趁夜逃脱，但误捕了中央提款委员易尔士（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">刘作抚</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>这就是中国现代史上有名的富田事变。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日当晚，事变领导人连夜召开紧急会议，认定李韶九所为是毛泽东的指示，毛是“许克祥第二”。为防止总前委派兵来，红军之间发生冲突，事变领导人决定红二十军西渡赣江，进驻永阳。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">第二天一早，红二十军在王家祠堂前的广场（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">富田广场</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）上召开士兵大会。被捕同志在会上报告了事件经过，控诉李韶九的恶行，有人还脱下衣服展示身上的伤痕……整个会场群情激愤，人们喊出了“打倒毛泽东，拥护朱（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">德</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）、彭（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">德怀</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）、黄（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">公略</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）”的口号。</SPAN></FONT></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>Ⅰ<SPAN lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN></SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>刘敌是在湖南马变（</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">马日事变——引者</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">）秋暴（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">秋收暴动——引者</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">）前加入党的，加入党以后没有一天脱离过工作和党，其中的斗争历史可以分作四小部分：<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>（一）十六年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>（<SPAN lang=EN-US>1927</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>年——引者</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>）冬在醴陵南三区任区委书记，过着盲动主义的生活。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>（四）伤愈，赣西南特委派敌到二十军工作，初任军委秘书长，九次攻吉（安）胜利后调一七四团政委，现任一七四团政委兼团委书记。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
