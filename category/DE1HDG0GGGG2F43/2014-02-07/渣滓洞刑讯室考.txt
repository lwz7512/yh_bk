<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">有一个流传极广的说法：“渣滓洞里有‘中美合作所’</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">48</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">套刑罚，凡是关押在渣滓洞的革命者，全都要经受酷刑折磨……”如果进一步了解有哪些革命者在渣滓洞受过刑？受的什么刑？受过几次刑？由哪几名特务施刑？受刑的后果是什么？则无人能准确回答。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>　　</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">孙曙问刑具的来历</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">孙曙是浙江诸暨人。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">18</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">岁的高中生孙曙与</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">位同学一起报考解放军第二野战军军大三分校，编入西南服务团第六支队。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月孙曙随部队进入重庆城，被分配在军管会公安部第二处内勤科担任侦察员。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，他们按照《接收匪特机构计划》，接管了国民党在西南的特务机构保密局、中统局。内勤科的任务是收集、汇编敌特人头及组织材料，编印《特勤汇编》。所有特务资料和缉凶信息全部汇集到内勤科。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日左右，孙曙到了渣滓洞，了解到在大屠杀后，男牢、看守用房等处经过了两次焚烧，第一次是特务看守用办公桌椅引火，第二次是由保密局西南特区行动纵队浇上酒精，焚烧后都已化为灰烬。他没有看见有焚余的刑具残骸。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1950</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年初，他从离白公馆不远的五灵观原军统“公产管理组”接管少量枪支和手铐等物品，还经手登记过缴获来的美式手枪、黄金等。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>孙曙非常肯定地对笔者说：“没有任何刑具。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>孙曙说的“刑具”，是指皮鞭、烙铁、电刑、老虎凳、竹签子之类逼供用的刑具。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1950</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年春节期间，在重庆大同路渝女师（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">现大同路小学</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）举办“磁器口大屠杀烈士遗物展览”，没有展出特务用刑的刑具。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月在西南公安部内勤科举办“肃特展览会”，展出</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">CMS</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">特勤机（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">军统潜伏电台</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、特务潜伏经费金条、无声手枪、钢笔手枪、一次性微型射击器等，也没有展出刑具。后来在西大公司、文化宫、大田湾举办公安展览，都未展出刑具。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">孙曙说，他在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1950</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年初接管的美式手铐，不是现在渣滓洞旧址陈列展出的这种手铐。他接管的那些手铐，两手之间的距离很近。他还非常肯定地说：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">50</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年代到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">60</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年代，重庆市公安局肯定没有缴获过大宗的“美国物品”。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2007</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日上午，笔者陪同当时</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">76</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">岁的孙曙去渣滓洞。老人在参观现场向笔者提问：现有这些展出用的“刑具”从何而来？当年谁见过“刑具”？</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">设立渣滓洞看守所的缘由</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">要对重庆行辕第二看守所（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312" lang=EN-US>1947</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">年<SPAN lang=EN-US>12</SPAN>月—<SPAN lang=EN-US>1949</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>11</SPAN>月，俗称“渣滓洞监狱”</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）从司法角度进行考察，首先要明确一些基本概念。最重要的是，渣滓洞看守所与“中美特种技术合作所”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312" lang=EN-US>1943</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">年<SPAN lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>月—<SPAN lang=EN-US>1946</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN>月</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）无关，也没有什么“中美合作所</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">48</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">套刑罚”。其次，看守所不负审讯之责，它只配合审讯。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">抗战胜利后，国民政府还都南京，重庆设为行辕。内战开始后，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1947</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年夏天改为绥靖公署，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年国共和谈，太平有望，又改为西南长官公署。名称虽变动，但组织内容基本未变，习惯上还是称“行辕”。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">行辕军法处设有“重庆行辕第一看守所”，主要关押刑事犯、寄押军事犯。地点在下半城原老巴县衙门监狱（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">今解放东路巴县衙门巷</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），其女监一直保存至</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">世纪初。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1947</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日深夜，国民党军警宪特进行全国性大逮捕，重庆被捕人数达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">260</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多人，各拘押点人满为患。据《民主报》记者唐弘仁回忆，他们被集中关押在枣子岚垭漱庐，普遍审讯后释放了一批，转移到石灰市稽查处看守所，又释放一批。最后留下</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">25</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人，加上从贵州转送来政治犯</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">余人，共</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">30</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多人，转押渣滓洞长期拘禁。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">正是为了长期监禁这批政治犯，当时公开与秘密身份分别为重庆行辕二处处长、保密局情报处少将处长的徐远举，决定设立行辕第二看守所。徐远举在《血手染红岩》中说，它是一个没有编制的单位，行政关系隶属于行辕二处（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">情报处</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；受行辕一处（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">军法处</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、保密局西南特区的指导和监督；业务上与保密局白公馆看守所、交警直属大队联系。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">任务为：配合审讯工作，负责秘密处决，负责看管并考核在押者之思想。看守原则为：男女分监，轻重分监，新旧在押人分监，重要者镣禁（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312" lang=EN-US>1954</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">年在押人员邓培新交代材料《渣滓洞情况》</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">“司法改良”却“有法不依”</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">看守所是国家司法体系的终端机构。根据民国《看守所组织条例》，其职责是看守监管，配合审讯（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">如提押犯人</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），看守所本身并不承担审讯之责。它需要配置镣铐戒具，但不应该有为逼问口供而设置的刑具如老虎凳、烙铁、竹签子等。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1900</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年八国联军入侵北京，签订不平等条约，其中“治外法权”的主要借口就是中国监狱管理野蛮落后，洋人不能受“土罪”，于是“监狱改良”成了政府需要解决的紧迫政治问题，列为清末新政的一部分。另一方面，在晚清民主思潮中，国家主权属于人民、法律面前人人平等的意识逐渐向社会普及，野蛮刑讯已经从法理上被摒弃，是监狱改良中首先要革除的行为。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1901</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">19</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日张之洞和刘坤一呈上关于监狱制度改良的奏折，提出编纂新法典、改良司法部门。晚清政府想通过司法改良来争取废除“治外法权”，换取列强承诺，这成了在国际背景下民族生存竞争能力的标志之一。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">孙中山于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1912</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月发布的《大总统令内务司法部通饬所属禁止体罚文》中规定，不论司法、内务各官署，在审理及判决民刑案时，不准再用笞杖（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">抽鞭子打板子</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、枷号及他项不法刑具。其罪当笞杖、枷号者，一律改为罚款和拘留。孙中山在《大总统令内务司法两部通饬所属禁止刑讯文》中规定“一概不准刑讯……从前不法刑具，悉令焚毁。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>建设“文明监狱”，以“隔离感化”代替“笞杖刑讯”，是近代中国司法改良的核心。从法理上说，如果看守无理地对犯人进行肉体折磨，也是违规。因此，在渣滓洞曾出现过这种情况：政治犯们理直气壮地一齐对看守怒吼“不许打人！”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2003</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年一个雨天，笔者参观“行辕第一看守所”即老巴县衙门监狱的女监，犹觉其阴暗潮湿，低矮狭小如关动物的囚笼。犯人直接睡在泥地上，冬天给几块木板当床。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月脱险志士金成林等人从渣滓洞押往第一看守所（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">准备由特刑庭公开审判</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），在这里被同牢的刑事犯欺负，躺在泥地上冻惨了，次日主动和刑事犯搞好关系，才被允许上木板去。之后通过交谈，刑事犯们对这几位政治犯十分佩服（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">脱险志士金成林于<SPAN lang=EN-US>2010</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>10</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>29</SPAN>日回忆</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">相比较而言，行辕第二看守所即渣滓洞，明显有一些“文明感化”设施，底楼及平房有上下铺床位，院墙上写有感化词，初期允许政治犯设立图书室、小卖部，还可打篮球。渣滓洞看守长李磊，表面上对政治犯态度和蔼，满口新名词，标榜自己是思想进步的新派人物。龙光章去世，他主动找政治犯搭话表示同情，听说难友们准备追悼会，“不仅同意，而且他还要参加并作主祭人”。就算非常虚伪，也说明当时确实在进行监狱改良。顺便说明，龙光章去世，难友们筹备追悼会时考虑过进行绝食斗争，但狱方同意开会，就无需绝食了（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">弗公《亲历者回忆“龙光章追悼会”经过》，《重庆渝中政协文史资料》第<SPAN lang=EN-US>20</SPAN>辑</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">当然，“感化”是一种理论上的理想境界，而理想与现实总是有距离的。看守所本质上是实施惩罚、限制自由的场所。如果犯人违反规定（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">或并没有违反规定</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）要受到惩罚，木板打手心、带重镣、关禁闭室，甚至遭到更加粗暴野蛮的对待。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">民国时期制定过两部“宪法”，一、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1912</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日由中华民国临时大总统孙中山主持制定并签署公布的《中华民国临时约法》；二、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1946</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月国民党召开“国民大会”制定并公布的《中华民国宪法》，同时《监狱组织条例》和《看守所组织条例》出台。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体"><FONT size=3>看守所组织条例<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>（民国三十五年一月十九日国民政府公布三十六年六月十日施行）<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第一条　看守所之设置<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>看守所隶属于所在地之地方法院检查处，关于审判中被告之羁押事项，并受法院之督导。地方法院辖区辽阔或案件繁多，得设二以上之看守所。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第二条　看守所之内部组织<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>看守所设戒护、卫生、总务三课。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第三条　戒护课掌握之事项<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>看守所戒备及被告之戒护；门户锁钥管理；管理员训练及勤务分配；武器、戒具、消防器之使用、练习及保管；被告之辅导、作业事项；被告饮食衣着卧具用品之分给及保管；卫生清洁事务之执行；接见、发受书信及送入物品之处理；被告性行考核及赏罚执行；被告押送及脱逃者追捕。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第四条　卫生课<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>看守所卫生计划及其设施与指导；传染病预防；被告健康检查；被告疾病医治；病室管理；药品调剂储备及医疗器械管理。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第五条　总务课<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>文件收发、撰拟及保存；印信典守；经费出纳；建筑修缮；被告入所出所；被告身份单、人相表、指纹之填制；携带物品之受付及保管；被告疾病死亡呈报及通知；被告粮食之收支、保管、核算及造报；不属于其他各课事项。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第六条　所长之职务<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>看守所置所长一人，兼任或委任；承监督长官之命，综理全所事务。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>首都、直辖市、省会所在地或容额在一千人以上之看守所，得设副所长一人……<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第七条　秘书<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>第八条　课长之职务<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>渣滓洞看守所的组织机构、职能作用、人员设置，几乎都能与该《组织条例》一一对应。最主要的不同在于隶属关系，渣滓洞不属法院管辖，它公开隶属于行辕二处，秘密隶属于保密局西南特区，且没有政府编制，相当于保密局内部看守所，这就为“法外施刑”提供了先决条件。</FONT></SPAN></P>
