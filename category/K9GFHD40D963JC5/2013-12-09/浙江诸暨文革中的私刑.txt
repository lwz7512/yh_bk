<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">去年，由于参与编写《中国共产党诸暨历史（二卷）》，在档案馆，我读到大量有关文革的档案，了解到当时的私刑可谓无奇不有，惨不忍睹。档案里把文革时期的私刑归纳为：批斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">游斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、动刑、体罚、人身侮辱、罚钱物等五类，为保持本来的文字面目，略作梳理之后照搬。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">一、私刑名目</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">批斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">游斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。一般在批斗者胸前挂一块上面写黑色姓名且在姓名上加红色大叉叉的黑牌，跪着斗，作喷气式（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">双手高举或向前平举</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）斗。挂上黑牌再附加挂上别的东西斗：如挂灭火机（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">说被斗者扑灭文化大革命的烈火</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、挂犁头铁（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">耕田用的铁犁的犁头，叫被斗者学老黄牛耕田。犁头每只<SPAN lang=EN-US>10</SPAN>多市斤，东和初中原党支部赵书记，癌病刚开过刀，挂了<SPAN lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>只犁头，<SPAN lang=EN-US>40</SPAN>多斤铁，用一条铁丝串起来挂在颈上，铁丝断了又结起来挂上，铁丝的结头跟铁丝一起嵌进肉里</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；挂一块涂上粪便的木头斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">双桥公社郦某数人，用一根从厕所粪坑边拆下来的木头，再从粪便中浸过挂在颈项上斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；挂二箱子弹斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">陈蔡区区长蒋某在诸暨第二次解放后胸前挂二箱子弹斗。作者按：诸暨的第一次解放指<SPAN lang=EN-US>1949</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>5</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>6</SPAN>日共产党赶走国民党，“第二次解放”指<SPAN lang=EN-US>1967</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>9</SPAN>月<SPAN lang=EN-US>14</SPAN>日一派从诸暨县城赶走另一派</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；挂二筐石子斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">草塔供销社共产党员、公社治保干部，都挂<SPAN lang=EN-US>80</SPAN>多斤两筐石子斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；挂步枪斗；挂门板斗；挂棺材的材头板斗（双桥公社原革委会王主任，就挂过材头板批斗）；挂臭牛尾巴斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">思安公社汤副书记，挂了腐烂发臭的牛尾巴斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；挂着抬枪斗（上峰公社党员干部俞同志，背七八十斤重的抬枪游斗）。戴古代官帽斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">县农田水利局干部翁某，戴上纸做的官帽，身穿戏装龙袍斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；戴一顶特制高帽斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">西山公社干部戴一顶三尺高的帽子，架着长竹竿牵着绳斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。自己敲锣，自己高喊罪名斗；跪在破碗片、破玻璃上斗；在煤渣上跪行到会场斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">化肥厂原党总支书记阮同志批斗时，命令他跪行几十米新铺煤渣路进会场，膝盖血肉和裤子嵌在一起</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；搭四层楼斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">诸暨化肥厂赵厂长，下面用台球桌，加方桌、课桌，再加凳子，凳上再放一根三角铁，跪在三角铁上斗，结果跌得半死</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。生病扶着斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">草塔公社平阔大队党张支书，患有严重肝炎，起不了床，而且一只脚跷，就把他抬到台上去批斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；重病躺着斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">保安公社毛家园大队民兵连毛连长，被造反派打成重伤，服药<SPAN lang=EN-US>80</SPAN>余剂起不来床，就抬到台上躺着斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；人死了，做成草人或木头人斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">诸暨化肥厂人保干部蒋同志被杀害后，做了一个会下跪、会低头的木头人斗</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；母婴分离斗（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">东和公社王六大队社员姚某，刚生下小孩子，第三天就被游斗，下身都是血，不准带小孩，不准喂奶，发现喂奶，夺下小孩，摔在地上</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。全家上斗台，第一个拿保护伞，后面跟一大串“牛鬼蛇神”斗；夫死斗妻；父死斗子（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">诸暨木器厂党总支书记老谢，在诸暨“第二次解放战争”中被枪杀，就将谢<SPAN lang=EN-US>13</SPAN>岁的孩子揪来替父斗，全家打成反革命，并被赶出门去以求乞为生</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">动刑。揿头，打耳光、拿皮鞋底打耳光，扭耳朵、夹子夹耳朵，夹子夹头发、揪头发、拔头发、用钳子拔头发，戴手铐、脚镣，皮鞭、铁链条抽打，沉粪缸（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">草塔区校男老师赵某、女老师周某，游斗后一起沉入粪缸</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），拗手骨、水泥地上推、摔“死人跌”、做单吊、老虎凳、板虎凳、踏脚尖、跪斗时踏脚跟、抓头发猛撞石板地板、针刺指头、刺刀犁胸、刺胸、灌水、阴道塞毛巾、浇开水（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">五泄公社泄溪大队社员杨某，用了<SPAN lang=EN-US>18</SPAN>壶开水从头上浇，烫死后把他丢入塘里，说是“自杀”的</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），反老鹰扑天飞、猴子捧桃、脖子盘蛇、盘练踏杠、铁丝烧红往手膀肉里穿进去、皮鞋脚踢胸骨、枪托笃腰骨、枪口笃腰子、铁棍笃肋骨、铁棍捅阴道、铁丝穿舌头（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">草塔木器厂共青团员沈某被毒打后用铁丝穿舌头致死</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），电刑（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">和济假案中，凶手对红马墩大队社员袁某用了<SPAN lang=EN-US>15</SPAN>节新电池接通他的左手动脉受刑</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、假枪毙，铁钉烧红后烫、刺膀臂（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">和济公社红马墩大队社员袁某的双臂都刺成残疾</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），竹片打屁股、打膝盖骨、酒精棉球烧着往衣袖里面塞、关进房子再用浓烟熏、钢条打耳打牙、手榴弹打、拔手指甲、拔脚趾甲、老龙喷水、尖刀戳、冰冻天气沉河塘、打得半死活埋、香烟火烫面孔，皮鞋踢、踩孕妇腹部，帽子里插针（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">五一公社李三大队批斗大队长李某时，一人做好了一顶里面插满大头针的帽子，戴在李的头上，以不老实为名，上台去揿头，刺得满头都是血</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、三上吊（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">一通宵吊三次，双手吊在梁上，脚尖点地</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、用枪射击（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">王家井大队社员楼某，“诸暨第二次解放”后有事到城关被抓进人民戏院里，要他面壁站，楼表示不满，武卫团就开枪射击打穿了大腿，成了残废</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），枪毙死尸（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">诸暨木器厂党总书记老谢被枪杀后，装进棺材，再用冲锋枪扫射</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）、掘坟，等等。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">体罚。坐，坐反省台、坐监狱、坐水牢、坐暗房、坐厕所、坐猪圈、坐牛栏、坐通宵（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">连续几夜不准睡觉</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；站，低头站，挂牌示众站，戴黑伞，剪头发示众站，烈日下站，冬天北风口站，霜雪地赤脚站，十字街口站、岔路口站，凳子上站，临时搭高台站，“牛鬼蛇神”一起站，监狱里整天站，贴壁站；跪，凳子、椅子上跪，碎玻璃、破碗片上跪，凳子朝天凳子脚上跪，口咬烂尸衣跪，举重跪，陪死尸跪，坟头窝跪，棺材前头跪，锅底上跪，冬天剥光衣服跪。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人身侮辱。罚吃酸饭，罚吃狗吃过的剩粥剩饭，罚吃糠拌饭（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">璜山公社社校党支书在当狗爬后，叫他吃糠拌饭</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），罚吃大便草纸（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">新壁公社几个干部被游斗到牌头公社，在烧好的青菜里拌进大便纸给他们吃</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），罚吃牛粪（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">硬塞</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），罚同四类分子一起劳动，罚新婚不准同房，罚钻狐狸洞，罚国庆节钻牛鬼蛇神洞，罚钻胯下（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">璜山批斗人民教师时，硬要女教师叉开双腿，命男教师钻女教师胯下</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">罚钱物。扣工分，扣工资，扣口粮；搜身（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">没收钞票、粮票</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），抄家（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">没收财、物</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）；封门、全家扫地出门，下放、全家下放，开除；掘灶头，捅瓦片，捅墙头，拆房屋；罚款，赔款、赔物，被吊打后还要给施刑者倒贴营养补贴费，坐监狱后再罚粮、罚钱。不给假期，不准就医。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">二、几个例子</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>在档案里还读到诸如《人民医院成了“地下监狱”》、《冯村大队的“三忠于室”》、《马剑风云》等许多材料，里面也涉及不少私刑，本文选录三则。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">《人民医院成了“地下监狱”》。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">“第二次解放”后，诸暨县人民医院成了“地下监狱”，病房变成“牢房”、“刑房”，任意抓人关押、施行各种酷刑。受刑者，不仅有医院的干部和医务人员，还有不同观点的伤病员。据不完全统计，在诸暨人民医院受过酷刑的有干部、职工</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">11</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人，医务工作人员</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">其中有<SPAN lang=EN-US>16</SPAN>个党员</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">），伤病员</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">50</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多人，至于外单位、外地抓去受刑的人员，那就无法统计了。在这个地下监狱里，所施的刑罚很多，闻所未闻。在用刑前先用胶布贴住眼睛，用换下来的带脓血的纱布塞住嘴巴，然后再用毒刑。有一个</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1941</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年参加革命后来在医院工作的老干部老党员陈某，由于观点不同，“第二次解放”后，一回到医院，就用胶布贴住眼睛，再用纱布扎牢，然后施刑，立时昏倒在地，之后把他从楼下倒着拖到楼上的一个房间里，脱光全身外衣，再毒打，再昏过去，等他醒来后，继续用更毒辣的刑罚——背甩，连甩数次，又昏过去，用冷水泼醒，关进一间小房子里，连续受刑三个小时，打得身上没有一块好肉，小便出血，水不能喝，饭不能吃，不能大小便，浑身疼痛不止。大西区委书记邵某，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1964</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年因患严重肝炎和肺病而住院，到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1967</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月上旬，因无人照料而出院。住院期间，由于观点不同，曾和一些人辩论过。“第二次解放”后，把他抓进人民医院，坐老虎凳、反元宝、拔头发、撞腰骨，残酷折磨</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">100</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多天，折磨厉害的有</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">次，皮开肉绽，内脏损伤，小便出血，病体加重，三次被打得昏死过去。一个姓傅的共产党员、复员军人，因腿部负伤住院，由于观点不同，“第二次解放”后，挂号住院，不但不给医治，反而将他关在一间冷房里，长达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">43</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">天，伤口腐烂出虫，臭不可闻，叫天天不应，叫地地不灵，没有办法，他只得将送饭的碗敲破，用破碗片把自己腿上的烂肉割掉，目睹者无不痛心，等折磨够了，赶出医院，成了残废。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">《冯村大队的“三忠于室”》。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">诸暨“第二次解放”后，平山公社冯村大队将大队会议室改装成金碧辉煌的“三忠于室”，用各色纸张制成各种字形的“三忠于”贴满四墙，桌上地上却放满了捆人的绳索、砍人的钢刀、打人的棍棒、做刑罚的大小板凳。就是这间“三忠于室”，到了深更半夜就成了人肉“加工场”，惨叫之声不绝于耳。村里有个无爹无娘姓戚的孤儿，因倾向于“老保”，就被加上“偷东西”的罪名，绑架进这个“三忠于室”，用绳索高高吊在房梁上，不料绳断人落，跌出一裤子大便，跌得半死。醒来后，造反派就强迫他“将大便用手捧出去”。戚姓孤儿只好挣扎着爬起来将大便用双手捧出屋外。回头又重新将这个无辜的小青年紧紧捆绑起来，继续他们的“三上吊”……就在这个金碧辉煌的“三忠于室”里，经受过吊打、跪斗、捆绑、三上吊及长时间关押的不同观点的群众达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">120</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多人，其中党员</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">14</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人，占该大队党员总数的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">70%</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">。在这里受过“教育”的人中，有不少人外逃而流落他乡，更有不少人终身致残。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">《马剑风云》。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">诸暨边远山区有个马剑公社，在文革中演出过二场别出心裁的《马剑风云》，“演员”全是这个公社的干部群众——生活里的真人。第一场：《钻牛鬼蛇神洞》。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1967</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月下旬的一天上午，在供销社门口地上用二层课桌搭成五个洞，洞口用马粪纸糊好，画上毒蛇，上书“牛鬼蛇神洞”五个大字，演出开始，造反派姜某（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">马剑中学一教师</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）手执话筒，登上“指挥台”，自任总指挥。他在台上高喊一声：“钻洞——”则早已被押送到场外的公社书记、中学校长等</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个党员干部，都得钻进那个“牛鬼蛇神洞”，然后在洞中坐定，作“牛鬼蛇神”当众展览。这一批“牛鬼蛇神”还必须得听姜某的训斥和咒骂，否则，旁边有打手队侍候。当时，也有干部因不甘受辱而拒绝钻洞，当场受刑吃眼前亏的。晚上继续演出，节目叫做《进洞出洞》，“牛鬼蛇神”强迫进洞后，点名某人出洞，这人就得揭发批判尚在洞内的“牛鬼蛇神”，揭批得不中意，就不许出洞。第二场：《攻堡垒》。第二天晚上，搭了两个“牛鬼蛇神洞”，称作两个“堡垒”，首先强迫</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个党员干部全部钻洞一遍，然后将公社人武部长费某与公社干部邱某等四人编成</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">号“目标”，分别站在两个“堡垒”中，低下头来等候“轰击”；再将公社书记沈某、妇女主任应某排列成</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">号炮手，分别组成两个炮组，各自找好轰击“目标”，恭候指挥台上姜某的发号施令。姜随口叫一声几组几号炮手“开炮”，那人就得遵命立即向自己的“目标开炮”，即所谓“揭发批判”。对上了姜的口味，姜就狂喊“嘉奖，嘉奖！”若稍有支吾，一声“滚下去！”，那人就大祸临头，拖下去就是一顿拳脚棍棒。姜某等人还将这种“戏”让马剑中学的边校长重点演出，在边校长宿舍门窗全部用白纸封掉，只在门中间挖出仅一个人能爬行的小洞，专供边爬进爬出。每次进出，碰破一点纸，就是“不老实”，就要拖去受刑。边校长前后爬洞</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">天，受刑不计其数。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>以上只是文革期间发生在浙江绍兴诸暨的私刑的不完全记录。笔者本人都曾亲身经受过二回，而且二回都是死里逃生。笔者也亲眼看见过自己的一位老师受刑的惨景。文革过去还不到半个世纪，文革的经历还有很多亲历者，文革的残酷不应被遗忘。</FONT></SPAN></P>
