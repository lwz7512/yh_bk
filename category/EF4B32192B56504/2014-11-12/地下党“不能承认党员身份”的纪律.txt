<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体"><FONT size=3>《如此中美特种技术合作所——蒋美特务重庆大屠杀之血录》第<SPAN lang=EN-US>37</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>页<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 15pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">“不能承认党员身份”相关纪律的形成</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 15pt"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>由于环境严酷，中共组织制定地下斗争策略非常细致，比较苛刻。地下党员们一旦被捕，是不能承认党员身份的。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">中共注意在实际斗争中掩饰党员身份，其历史可以追溯到土地革命时期。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1928</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年至</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1931</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年周恩来在上海负责隐蔽战线工作时，十分强调组织活动的隐蔽性，要求党员们单线联系，尽量职业化、社会化。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1940</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，毛泽东起草指示《放手发展抗日力量，抵抗反共顽固派的进攻》，提出了地下党活动原则“隐蔽精干，长期埋伏，积蓄力量，以待时机”，经中央政治局会议讨论通过，作为党在国统区工作的“十六字方针”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">严格说来，“长期埋伏，积蓄力量，以待时机”是方针；“隐蔽精干”是政策；勤业、勤学、勤交友及职业化、社会化、合法化的“三勤三化”是方法</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">抗战期间，周恩来多次指示南方局机关工作人员和下属各地党组织，一旦有党员被捕，马上紧急处置，迅速隔离，割断关系。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1941</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>17</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，国民党中央通讯社公布对“皖南事变”处置的通令，当天晚上，周恩来在重庆红岩村八路军办事处二楼的过道中，向全体工作人员说：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">……我们一定要作最坏的准备，要准备反动派可能搞突然袭击。他袭击我们，主要是要搞我们党的机密：密码、文件、地下党员名单等。因此，必要时我们也要进行适当的抵抗，把机密文件毁完了，就准备坐牢。……他们要问你们是不是共产党员，男同志都承认是共产党员，女同志承认是家属，因为我们是公开的共产党机关。问你们党的组织情况，就说我们的中央在延安，主席是毛泽东，这里有支部，书记是周恩来。再问还有哪些负责人，就说有董必武、邓颖超。再要问就说不知道，让他们问支部书记周恩来。我们在牢里，要坚持不泄露党的机密，好好保养身体。……</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>《周恩来传》第<SPAN lang=EN-US>488</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>页，中央文献出版社</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1989</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>周恩来在紧急情况之下的这个讲话，提出了对付敌人逮捕审讯方面的一个重要原则，即国共合作期间，在国统区公开的中共机关的男同志可以承认党员身份，但女同志不要承认。以此类推，地下党的同志一律不要承认党员身份。周恩来不仅顾及党的机密这一最高原则，同时也很人性化地指出，党员们应该很好地保护自己，尽量减少损失，不要做无谓的牺牲。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>之后，周恩来指示成立一个秘密工作委员会，制定《保密工作条例》，明确规定了若干安全措施，已成惯例的“夫妻之间不能谈论党的机密”，就是这个《条例》中制定的正式纪律。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1947</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月，国共合作彻底破裂，国统区没有公开的中共机关了，“不能承认中共党员身份”成为国统区地下党员们被捕后应付审讯的主要原则之一。因为，在审讯中，被捕者只要承认了一点，紧跟着敌人便会提出第二点来逼迫承认，一步一步紧追不舍。因此，地下党员们在审讯中必须把抵御的防线牢牢地构筑在第一道关口，一问三不知。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月《国民公报》连载杨祖之的文章《我从集中营出来》，在其“鬼门关”一节中，从实际情况出发对这一点进行了解释。由于当时杨祖之还不是中共党员，他是从“进步青年”的角度来理解和说明的：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>……你承认自己“进步”，他必定叫你承认“思想左倾”；你承认希望“政治民主”，那你“思想左倾”已有铁证，他必定要你交出共产党组织！因为共产党才谈这个。不说，就是不坦白。这简直成了他们的公式。他们对犯人的要求，是无止尽的……<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>再看因《挺进报》被捕的陈然烈士的审讯记录，堪称“隐蔽身份”的经典：<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>徐远举：你就是陈然！把你的组织交出来吧！<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>陈然：办报是自由职业，有什么组织不组织，不让办，不办就是了，交什么组织？<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>徐远举：好一个自由职业！谁叫你办的？说吧。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>陈然：办报有什么罪？有这么严重！<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>徐远举：你办报，为什么不登记？为什么偷着办？老实告诉你，你的全部材料，已经有人交了出来，你还不交组织？<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>陈然：没有登记，现在登记也不迟。至于说到有什么人交出材料，那不是很好吗？那还要我交什么材料！<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>徐远举：你在强辩！你知道这是什么地方？你今天要听我的，我看你有什么本领不交组织？<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>陈然：不交又怎么样？<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>徐远举：不交，就强迫你交！<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">陈然：那你就强迫吧！</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>《陈然烈士传略》蒋一苇等著，重庆出版社<SPAN lang=EN-US>1983</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; <P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 15pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">“承认党员身份”至少是违纪</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 15pt"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>当年的地下党员全都明白，按照周恩来亲口布置的、不成文的地下工作纪律，“承认党员身份”属于变节行为，至少是违纪，严重的甚至可以上升为背叛。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>由谭竹安填写的江竹筠“被难烈士登记表”中，实事求是地填写“结果只承认了自己的立场”，就是说江竹筠承认了自己是共产党员。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">在《如此中美特种技术合作所——蒋美特务重庆大屠杀之血录》纪念册第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>37</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">页“被难烈士事略”中，为避英雄讳，画蛇添足地加上“这个承认是经过了渣滓洞全体难友通过而请求她承认的”，为之后《红岩》小说的创作扫清障碍，使“江姐”这一人物原型顺利通过艺术加工。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">同样在毒刑之下“承认党员身份”的，还有殉难于渣滓洞的女烈士邓惠中，邓惠中迟至</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1983</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年才被评定为烈士。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>80</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年代初期为了落实“未定烈士”问题，中共四川省委组织部派出调查组，赴四川南充地区了解邓惠中的狱中表现，找到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1948</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年参加过侦讯邓惠中的岳池县军法室人员。根据其提供的证明材料，邓惠中在毒刑之下承认自己是中共党员，而据此做出的组织结论，也认为承认共产党员身份是犯错误，但为可以原谅的错误：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">被询问者供述</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">……当时我们千方百计去感化她，但邓惠中却瞧不起，说：我几十年没有见到国民党，只见到共产党人。我们见感化不行就施以毒刑，在这种情况下邓承认自己是共产党员。……结论：邓没有损害党的言行，表现坚强，在严刑拷打下承认自己是共产党员……</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">据四川南充地区档案馆资料</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>更典型的一个例子是殉难于白公馆的刘国鋕烈士。刘国鋕的侄子刘以治写了《回忆罗广斌》一文，其中提到刘国鋕在烈士级别序列中的位置最低，还差一点儿就评不上烈士了，原因也在于他承认了自己的共产党员身份：</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>……我刚到重庆的时候罗广斌正忙于为“<SPAN lang=EN-US>11</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>·</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>27</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>”死难烈士筹办追悼会。有一天他兴致冲冲地跑来找我，叫我给他一张叔（即刘国</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; <P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1950</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>15</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，重庆市各界举行“追悼杨虎城将军暨被难烈士大会”，会后印发了一本纪念册《如此中美特种技术合作所——蒋美特务重庆大屠杀之血录》（</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>小<SPAN lang=EN-US>16</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>开本，内页共</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>84</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>页，繁体字竖排</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。在其“被难烈士事略”中，第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>37</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">页对江竹筠烈士的介绍有这样一段话：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>一九四八年六月十四号，在万县被捕，当晚在万县警察局严刑拷讯，始终没有说出任何东西。解到重庆中美合作所后，特务们一点不放松她，戴重镣，坐老虎凳，吊鸭儿浮水，夹手指……极刑拷讯中，曾经昏死过三次，但她只承认自己是个候补党员。而这个承认是经过了渣滓洞全体难友通过而请求她承认的。<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>最后两句非常费解——“她只承认自己是个候补党员。而这个承认是经过了渣滓洞全体难友通过而请求她承认的。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>为什么要加上这么两句？</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>承认自己是“候补党员”，比承认自己是“正式党员”，程度上轻微一些吗？</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">读者实难明白，被牢房高墙隔离着的渣滓洞全体难友，是怎样“通过”这一提议，然后在怎样的境况下“请求她承认”的？江竹筠在渣滓洞刑讯室里受刑（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">竹筷子夹手指</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）只有一次，当她正忍受煎熬死去活来时，渣滓洞全体难友正在开会？会议决定，为减轻痛苦，请求江竹筠承认自己是中共党员？为了程度上的轻微，只承认自己是“候补党员”而不能承认是“正式党员”？这个决定是由谁、以何种方式传达给江竹筠的？</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">还有另一个印证。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年秋天，在重庆白公馆、渣滓洞系列大屠杀中被害的殉难者，大多数都由其亲友填写过一份“被难烈士登记表”，交给“烈士资格审查委员会”，以此作为重要的烈士评审依据。</SPAN></FONT></P>
