<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　从媒体报道得知，我所揭露的情况仅是冰山一角。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　据《中国证券报》记者调查，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1998</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年房改以后，福利分房虽被明令禁止，但公务员系统内的变相福利分房一直没有停止过。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　网上可以查到北京中直机关和国务院机关福利分房的种种方式和具体案例，现选择几个案例如下：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　变相福利分房方式之一：低价团体购买商品房分给公务员。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　案例：禧福汇国际社区（外交部团购）。地点，双井桥东南。团购价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米，新房市场价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">33000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。周边二手房价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">30000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　案例：京投快线·阳光花园（市政府团购）。地点，丰台区大红门西路。团购价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。新房市场价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">25800</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。周边二手房价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">23000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　案例：北京朝阳新城两限房项目——金隅景和园三栋楼座被农业部定向团购，数百套房源将以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元卖给内部人员。该房市场价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">40000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米，周边二手房价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">15000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米左右。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　变相福利分房之方式二：公务员瓜分困难户的经济适用房。前些年北京市盖了一大批经济适用房，应当是以低于市场价（</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多元／平方米）卖给低收入困难家庭的。普通的困难群众申请保障房都要经过严格的审批，而这些经济适用房中相当一部分被不具备低收入条件的公务员们以低价购买。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　变相福利分房方式三：国家单位集资自建房分给公务员。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　案例：市发改委建委集资房。地点，六里桥西局，均价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米，市场价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">30000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。周边楼盘：永翌公馆：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">29800</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米；第三区：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">28000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米；周边二手房参考价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">25000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　案例：人民银行集资房。地点，宣武门康乐里，均价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米，市场价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">50000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。周边楼盘：四合上院：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">33000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米；中信城：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">28000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米；周边二手房参考价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">30000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　案例：市铁路局集资房。位置广安门手帕口南，均价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">5000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米，市场价</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">32000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。周边楼盘：红山世家：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">40000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米；周边二手房参考价：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">30000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">元／平方米。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　《</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">世纪经济报道》</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2010</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">24</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日发表的报道称：经记者调查，网上揭露上述情况基本属实。北京东三环的和谐雅园，西三环益丰苑小区，西直门某铁路住宅小区，职工内部价格远远低于市场价格。住房和城乡建设部研究中心副主任王珏林告诉《</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">世纪经济报道》记者，这些楼盘能建起来，要么是获得了划拨或者转让土地，要么是在自己土地上盖房，因为如果是走商品房路子，这个土地要招拍挂，显然是不可能那么低价。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　继国发</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1998</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">【</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">27</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">】号文件以后，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2003</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月建设部、监察部、国土资源部三部委联合发出通知，严禁党政机关利用职权或其影响，以任何名义、任何方式搞集资合作建房。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2007</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">平方米／套，且要有严格的准入条件，配置过程要对社会公开透明。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　然而，一方面禁令不断，一方面大批公务员住进了豪华的“经济适用住房”。北京华远集团总裁任志强在接受媒体采访时称，在北京的政策性住房里，不公开、不透明、不面向社会的，大概要占到四分之三，面向社会大概只有四分之一。任志强说的这些“不公开、不透明的政策性住房”，其中相当大的一部分以“经济适用房”的低价卖给了公务员。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">公务员福利分房危害极大</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></B></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　本刊发表《住房改革的由来和现状》后，城乡建设部政策研究中心主任陈淮很快就在《经济参考报》发表了题为《公务员住房不应享受超国民待遇》的文章。该文指出，“这些都是给公务员超国民待遇的腐败做法”。“在世界各国，公务员群体因政策保障而在当地工薪阶层中享受最好居住条件的，不论富国还是穷国，不论大城市还是小城镇，不论历史还是现实，除了前苏联，一个例证都找不到”，“一些国家历史上在公务员住房问题上的不慎做法曾引发严重的社会危机以至政治危机，其教训不可谓不深刻。”</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　有读者给本刊来信认为，中直机关和国务院机关违反国务院发的文件，直接挑战了国家权威，这将造成十分危险的后果。连中直机关和国务院直属单位都不执行国务院的文件，谁还会执行中央法令？令不行、禁不止的无政府状况有可能成为常态。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　有媒体指出，在住房市场化十多年后，还给公务员福利分房，这是有组织的大规模的群体腐败。在房价高攀以后，近来房租又连连高升，大批无房者被挤进“蜗居”、“胶囊”，大学生成了“蚁族”，买房者成了“房奴”。在这种情况下，公务员还享受福利分房，造成了强烈的社会反差，将会激化社会矛盾，破坏社会和谐。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　有学者在媒体上著文表示，公务员福利分房，挤占了按市场行为经营的企业的市场空间，侵占了公共资源、侵吞国有资产，用公权为部分社会成员谋取私利。离权力中心越近，得到的资源就越大，资源分配机制就越有保障。这是权力腐败的表现。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></P>
