<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>《说帖》同时说明日方的主要论据：</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1896</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年，明治天皇曾以第十三号敕令，将尖阁群岛划归冲绳县八重山郡石垣村所属，在</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1931</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年，再以天皇敕令予以确认。但是，魏道明也指出，日方所主张之各项论据，“表面上似颇充实，但我在事实仍有充分之理由予以驳复”。因为在</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1879</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年日本吞并琉球时，“并未包括尖阁群岛，直至</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1895</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年与清廷签订马关条约取得台、澎之主权后，始发表该项敕令”。可见，这是“日本取得琉球、台、澎后之一项内政处分”，此前钓鱼岛并非属于琉球，否则自无“另行发表敕令”的必要。</FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1969</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年，美国总统尼克松与日本首相佐藤荣作之间曾达协议，将琉球“复归”日本。蒋介石裁定钓鱼岛主权问题的当日，台湾当局发表声明，首先表示不能接受</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1969</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年美、日两国之间的协议。《声明》说：</FONT></SPAN></P>
