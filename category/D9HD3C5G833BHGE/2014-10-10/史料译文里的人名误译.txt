<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>161</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">号文件，即皖南事变发生之后不久，中共中央致季米特洛夫的一份电报，其中第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">项说：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">（</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">1941</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">年</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>）<SPAN lang=EN-US>2</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>月</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>22</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>日晚上，中国小党开会，未邀请我们参加。出席会议的有：国家社会党和青年党的首领，黄炎培（</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">职业教育社</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">）、梁漱溟（乡村教育会）、张表方（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">即张澜，四川集团</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">）、章伯钧（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">第三党和救国会）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">、张石宽（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">音，来自宁夏</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">）。他们讨论了他们对国民参政会的态度和国共之间的冲突问题。</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>中共党史出版社，<SPAN lang=EN-US>2012</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>年版，第</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>159</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>页</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>这张石宽是什么人，从来没有听说过。想来是一位当时的政治态度和黄炎培、梁漱溟、张澜、章伯钧他们相同至少相近的对共产党友好的国民参政员。于是我就试着去查找相关材料，在“第一届国民参政会参政员名单”里看到“宁夏省：周士观”（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>见《国民参政会纪实》上卷，第<SPAN lang=EN-US>67</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>页，重庆出版社，</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1985</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>年版</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>），在“第二届国民参政会参政员名单”里又看到“宁夏省一名：周士观”（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>见《国民参政会纪实》下卷，第<SPAN lang=EN-US>783</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>页</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。很可能“张石宽”就是“周士观”的误译了。关于这个人的情况，《国民参政会纪实》（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">续编</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）里的《参政员简介》是这样介绍他的：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>周士观宁夏省平罗县人，<SPAN lang=EN-US>1893</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>年生。美国威斯康辛大学硕士。曾经任宁夏省政府驻京代表，第十五路军总指挥部参议，第十七集团军驻汉办事处处长，宁夏省政府驻渝办事处处长。</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1938</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>年</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>6</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>月至</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1945</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>年任第一届、第二届、第三届国民参政会参政员。</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1945</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>年任民主建国会中央常务委员。中华人民共和国成立后，任全国政协委员、民建中央常委兼组织委员会副主任委员。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>重庆出版社，<SPAN lang=EN-US>1987</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>年版，第</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>353</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>页</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">《周恩来年谱》（</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">1898</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>—<SPAN lang=EN-US>1949</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>·修订本</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1941</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日条：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>在玉皇观同黄炎培、周士观、沈钧儒、邹韬奋、章伯钧、张申府、左舜生、张君劢商量对国民参政会的态度。事后，周恩来将聚谈情况电告毛泽东，主张接受他们的建议：以中共七参政员名义将中共提出的十二条善后办法提到参政会要求讨论，以此作为出席参政会的条件，否则不能出席；成立各党派委员会，讨论国共关系和民主问题，在此会上提出“十二条”。<SPAN lang=EN-US>14</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>日中共中央书记处来电同意，并同意在参政会外成立各党派委员会讨论政治问题。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>中央文献出版社，<SPAN lang=EN-US>1998</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>年版，第</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>503</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>页</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>这个记载正好印证中共中央致季米特洛夫的那一份电报。</FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312"><FONT size=3>陈宏谟报告说，他与毛泽东进行了第二次谈话，持续约四个小时，并且毛泽东指出了十个方面，其内容可归结为：（<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>）中共赞成团结，支持蒋介石和中央政府的行政命令，目的是加强解放战争的实际力量；（</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>2</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>）中共赞成国家政策和进行解放战争，它将坚决把解放战争进行到底；（</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>3</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>）如果陈群放弃反共政策，中共就停止反对他的宣传；……</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>同上书，第<SPAN lang=EN-US>233</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>～</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>234</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>页</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">这里又一次出现了“陈群”这姓名。不过这一次我有办法查找了。因为这是在毛泽东和陈宏谟的谈话中说到的。于是我就来查阅《毛泽东年谱》。在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1941</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日这一条里看到：</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 楷体_GB2312">同国民党联络参谋陈宏谟、郭亚生、周励武谈话四小时，陈等表示蒋介石、何应钦、张治中、吴铁城在国共关系上均愿转圜，要求朱德到重庆去一次。毛泽东告诉他们以下几点：（一）国民党方面释放叶挺，共产党方面即派董必武一人出席参政会，否则仍不能出席。（二）共产党决不推翻国民党政府，决不越过现有疆界线，国民党承认共产党在敌后有发展权利，承认现有防地，承认边区；至于在敌后的国共两军，双方下令互不攻击。（三）恢复新四军，发给欠饷，停止逮捕。（四）何应钦停止反共，共产党即停止反何，并可重新来往，但何若再反共，共产党必再反何。……</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>《毛泽东年谱》（<SPAN lang=EN-US>1893</SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3>—</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1949</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>），中卷，中央文献出版社，</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>1993</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>年版，第</FONT><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3>325</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3>页</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>看了《毛泽东年谱》的记载，就知道那个“陈群”必定是何应钦的误译了。至于为什么会把三个音节的“何应钦”错译成两个音节的“陈群”，这原因我想不出来。</FONT></SPAN></P>
