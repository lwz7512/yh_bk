<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>为中国社科院翻译《雅科夫列夫访谈录》</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: Dotum; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Dotum" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">АЛЕКСАНДР ЯКОВЛЕВ/ ИЗБРАННЫЕ </FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: Dotum; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-fareast-font-family: Dotum" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">ИНТЕРВЬЮ: 1992-2005</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: Dotum; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-hansi-font-family: Dotum">以下</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">简称</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-hansi-font-family: Dotum">《</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">访谈录</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-hansi-font-family: Dotum">》</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: Dotum; FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>犹如苦熬“精神炼狱”。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>全世界都知道戈尔巴乔夫，知道他的“公开性”、“新思维”、“改革”，却很少有人知道“公开性”的开创者、“新思维”的“灰衣主教”、“改革”的设计师——雅科夫列夫</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">（<SPAN lang=EN-US>1923</SPAN>年<SPAN lang=EN-US>-2005</SPAN>年）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>。两者虽然都是布尔什维克制度的叛逆者，但前者更像是替罪羊，后者才是掘墓人。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>一部《红楼梦》早就告诉我们，荣国府的衰败既怨不得“总经理”王熙凤，也怨不得“董事长”贾政。那个造就薛蟠，也造就贾宝玉的腐朽制度，灭亡是历史的必然。雅科夫列夫认为，斯大林统治下的所谓“苏维埃社会主义制度”实质是带有奴隶制遗传基因的专制制度，这个制度的灭亡也是历史的必然。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>发人深省的是，像雅科夫列夫那样一个为保卫苏维埃祖国出生入死的人，一个在布尔什维克党培养下从权力金字塔底层爬到顶峰的人，一个曾经真诚信仰马列主义并为苏维埃政权尽心效力的人，一个既享受过一切“党内特权”又经历过“高处不胜寒”的人，怎么就从苏共宠儿变成了苏共的掘墓人呢？</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">《访谈录》似乎为我们提供了某种答案。《访谈录》选录的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">100</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">篇谈话，是作者从</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1992</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2005</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年对俄罗斯各新闻媒体谈话中具有代表性的一部分，它所提供的有关苏共和苏联的历史档案信息，在数量、广度和深度上都令人震惊。尤其因为雅科夫列夫既是苏俄的著名历史学家，又是戈尔巴乔夫时代主导“民主改革”的政治局委员，还是戈尔巴乔夫和叶利钦两位总统的“国师”，且一直担任“为政治迫害受害者平反委员会”主席，接触大量已经解密和尚未解密的苏联历史档案，所以，他所披露的档案史料具有毋庸置疑的可信度。你可以对他的观点说“不”，但你无法对档案袋里现身的历史真相说“否”。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">令人震撼的首先还是斯大林及其专制制度的暴行。笔者从开始工作的第一天起就与苏联结下不解之缘，还在那里先后当了</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年多的常驻记者，算得上是对苏共历史并不陌生的人，但《访谈录》披露的许多内幕仍然让我不寒而栗。斯大林及其体制迫害的对象不仅仅是一个又一个“反党集团”和一批又一批“人民的敌人”，而且是数以百万计的农民、工人、军人、知识分子、妇女、儿童、旧官吏、旧军人及其家属、被俘的红军官兵、少数民族普通百姓……受害者总数达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万之众，占战后人口的三分之一。这是无法用任何“扩大化”可以解释的。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">据《访谈录》引述的苏共秘密档案记录，斯大林本人曾亲手签发</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">366</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">份枪毙名单，直接剥夺</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4.4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万人的生存权利，这还不包括被其政策迫害致死的数百万牺牲者。他“既不认识他们，又不看他们的立案材料，就签发枪毙名单”——显然，这与“政策失误”风马牛不相及。</SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">受迫害的“祖国叛徒”和“人民的敌人”的家属计达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">40056</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1938</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日斯大林和莫洛托夫批准了叶若夫提供的一份名单，对</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">15</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">名“人民的敌人”的妻子提起“一级”诉讼，她们全部被枪毙，其中</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人是家庭主妇，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人是大学生。她们的丈夫在稍后的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1939</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年初统统被枪决。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">令人发指的是，契卡和克格勃人员竟然把签署“枪毙”名单当作“社会主义劳动竞赛”，比谁签得快、签得多</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">,</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">不走任何司法程序，闭着眼睛签发枪毙名单。内务人民委员部副部长弗里诺夫斯基受斯大林委派乘火车去远东，带了</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">名助手和所谓“相册”——装订成册的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">幅“照片”。他们边喝酒，边听留声机，边搞竞赛：看谁签署——就是在判决书上写上字母“р”</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">（俄文</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 仿宋_GB2312" lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">расстрел</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">即枪毙一词的第一个字母——笔者注）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>的案卷最多。他所带的全部案卷所涉及的人悉数被枪决。在内务部三局和四局之间也搞了社会主义竞赛。竞赛结果写道：“四局抓得多，三局杀得多”，四局胜出</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">（《访谈录》，№<SPAN lang=EN-US>82</SPAN>）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">杀人是有指标的。一份地方长官给中央的电报说：“我们请求补充指标。”为完成上面下达的“枪毙指标”，日托米尔州一个叫维亚特金的人，不经侦讯和审判就擅自决定对</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人执行枪决。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>杀人无需理由。雅科夫列夫说：“不久前我读到一份个人案卷——吉娜伊达·阿德米拉尔斯卡娅。她是伊万诺沃的纺织女工。上面指示要从工人中提拔一名做共青团工作的干部。她被选为伊万诺沃州共青团第一书记。克格勃把她叫去，向她出示一份名单，说：你听好了，这些人依你看正在策划一项针对党和政府领导人、针对斯大林的恐怖行动。你来充当他们的头头，向我们揭发他们都是你的阴谋同伙，然后我们把你从这个案子中摘出来。她拒绝这样做。结果她被抓起来枪毙了。临刑前她要了一面小镜子，对着镜子整理头发，以便死得有尊严。这件事太让我震撼。这些替身后面，这样被枪杀的人，不计其数。”</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">（《访谈录》，№<SPAN lang=EN-US>83</SPAN>）</SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>鲜为人知的是，还有数以百万计的孩子沦为牺牲品。他们有罪仅仅因为他们是贵族、“富农”、神职人员和所有被列入“人民的敌人”大名单的人的子女，是德国人、车臣人、卡尔梅克人、克里米亚鞑靼人……所生的孩子。</FONT></SPAN></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月“特殊村民”即受迫害的少数民族居民总数达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2092527</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人，其中</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">岁以下儿童为</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">755278</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人。总体死亡率达</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">27%</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，其中儿童死亡率比成年人要高</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">（《访谈录》，№<SPAN lang=EN-US>53</SPAN>）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">雅科夫列夫说，在不到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">40</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年的时间里，布尔什维克政权以“革命”的名义扼杀了</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万人的生命</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">（包括卫国战争中牺牲的<SPAN lang=EN-US>3000</SPAN>万）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，这是不可饶恕的罪行。崇尚权力的统治者和崇尚暴力的专制制度假“无产阶级专政”之名行法西斯暴政之实，这与真正的共产党人和科学社会主义能有什么关系呢？</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">《访谈录》披露的另一重大历史事实是“莫洛托夫</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">-</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">里宾特洛甫和约”及其隐瞒了数十年的两个秘密备忘录。这些文件虽然在西方早有爆料，但在苏联却半个世纪讳莫如深。在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1989</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月第二次苏联人民代表大会上，雅科夫列夫做了关于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1939</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年“苏德互不侵犯条约”</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">（即“莫洛托夫—里宾特洛甫和约”）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">及其附属秘密备忘录签署后果的报告。代表大会首次做出承认秘密备忘录存在的决议</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">（原件直到<SPAN lang=EN-US>1992</SPAN>年秋天才找到）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，并对这些文件的签署进行了严厉谴责。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">“条约”及其附件是二战前苏德勾结，合谋瓜分波兰和欧洲的铁证。正是根据这一“条约”，德国从北、南、西三面入侵波兰，苏联从东部占领波兰</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">（包括制造屠杀<SPAN lang=EN-US>22000</SPAN>名波兰军人的卡廷惨案）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，同时并吞斯堪的纳维亚半岛三国和芬兰、罗马尼亚的一部分，后来，又迫使外蒙古脱离中国变成苏联的缓冲国。斯大林还以盟友的身份向希特勒提供石油和粮食援助，甚至命令欧洲共产党不得支持本国政府抵抗德寇。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">由于斯大林过分相信希特勒的“友谊”，使苏联对德国“背信弃义”发动闪电战猝不及防，从战争一开始就溃不成军，致使</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">500</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万苏联红军在短短</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">100</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">天内全军覆没，一半被歼灭，一半被俘虏</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">（据苏军总参谋部的资料记载，在<SPAN lang=EN-US>4</SPAN>年卫国战争中被俘的苏联官兵总数为<SPAN lang=EN-US>405.9</SPAN>万，而在头两年被俘的就有<SPAN lang=EN-US>333.9</SPAN>万人，占被俘总数的<SPAN lang=EN-US>82%</SPAN>）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，进而导致</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万人死于卫国战争的巨大悲剧</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312; FONT-SIZE: 9pt">（《访谈录》，№<SPAN lang=EN-US>100</SPAN>）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">雅科夫列夫说，许多关于“为了斯大林，冲啊！”的神话都是马屁文人的异想天开。作为卫国战争老战士，他本人没有也从未听到过别人在战场上高喊什么口号，“生死关头唯一的本能反应就是一定要抢在被敌人消灭之前消灭敌人”。所以，用</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3000</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">万祖国优秀儿女</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">（其中多数是健壮的青年）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">的生命换来的最终胜利为斯大林编织桂冠是苏共的耻辱。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">《访谈录》对</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1917</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年“十月革命”真相的爆料是颠覆性的。我们从孩提时代就铭记在心的一句话：“十月革命一声炮响给中国传来了马列主义”，原来“十月革命”竟是一场未放一枪的宫廷政变：既不曾有“阿芙乐尔号巡洋舰”的炮声，也不曾有工农武装“攻打冬宫”，有的只是列宁的卫队长带领十几个人，朝天花板放了一枪，像抓小鸡一样地抓捕了临时政府的部长们。列宁挥动手臂说“一向所说的工农革命实现了！”的场面只不过是“列宁在十月”的电影家们的“艺术想象”。</SPAN></FONT></P>[FS:PAGE]
