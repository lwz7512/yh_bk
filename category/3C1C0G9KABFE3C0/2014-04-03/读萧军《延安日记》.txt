<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal align=center></FONT></o:p></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体"><FONT size=3>萧军延安日记影印件<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></FONT></SPAN></P>

<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri><BR>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1940</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>15</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，萧军来到延安，这是他第二次来延安。第一次是两年前（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312" lang=EN-US>1938</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">年<SPAN lang=EN-US>3</SPAN>月</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）的短暂停留，而这一次，他在延安一住就是六年，直到抗战胜利后的</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1946</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年，萧军一家随“鲁艺”前往东北解放区，告别了延安和中共中央。这六年时间对萧军而言，是他人生道路上的一段重要时期；对中共历史而言，是中共发展史上的关键时期。萧军在此期间写下了内容丰富的日记（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">萧军著：《延安日记（<SPAN lang=EN-US>1940</SPAN>—<SPAN lang=EN-US>1945</SPAN>）》（上下卷）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">，香港：牛津大学出版社，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>2013</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">。以下简称《日记》，引用只</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast">注</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">明上下卷和页码）。《日记》中大部分材料未曾披露、足够新鲜，对于通视中共政治文化传统的建立具有不可忽视的重要价值。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 16pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">对延安等级文化和不良现象的批评遍及《日记》笔墨</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">作为一个作家，萧军的心灵是非常细腻的。他刚到延安就发现，这里开始制造“谄上”的传统：“一些人不是在革命，不是在工作，完全把自己的地位造起来，然后慢慢地爬”，萧军认为这是“新兴的官僚主义”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>1</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">有一次他开会回来：“忽然一辆汽车呜呜地叫着从人群后面冲过来了，从人群中冲开了一条路……去了。当时使我感觉到好像从我的心上这样冲开了一条路，我和这坐汽车的人有了区别。我知道这是毛泽东的，但我不知道这车里坐的什么人？”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">卷上页<SPAN lang=EN-US>162</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）还有一次，萧军到河西山谷玩，“遇见毛泽东的老婆骑在马上，跑着去高级干部休养所去了”，他不禁联想，“这里连个作家休养所也没有，无论哪里的特权者，总是选择最好的肉给自己吃的”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>175</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>萧军看望住院的爱人王德芬的时候，听到在他看来是“很多丑恶的事情”：“李伯钊</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="POSITION: relative; LETTER-SPACING: -0.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; TOP: -3pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-text-raise: 3.0pt">注</SPAN><FONT face=Calibri><SPAN style="POSITION: relative; LETTER-SPACING: -0.5pt; FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; TOP: -3pt; mso-text-raise: 3.0pt" lang=EN-US>1</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes"><FONT size=3>&nbsp;</FONT></SPAN></SPAN></FONT><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">自带小鬼，每天做饭五次，罐头、牛乳、鸡蛋、香肠等应有尽有，馒头也是白的”，“杨尚昆买鸡蛋总是成筐的，每天早晨以牛乳，鸡蛋，饼干代早餐”；“毛泽东女人生产时，不独自带看护，而且门前有持枪卫兵。产后大宴宾客。去看病人时，总是坐汽车一直开进去，并不按时间……”萧军慨叹“卑污地存在原来到处一样……中国革命的路还是遥远的。”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>200</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">萧军对延安日益明显的个人崇拜现象非常不以为然。他某天“去马列学院参观联共党史参考资料……有几张照片，故意牵强附会以及把列宁、史塔林的照相特别放大，这使我反感。不禁想到这些政治追随者，只有政治和政治领袖，不会再想到别人。中国也正在进行这现象，这是奴性的表现，我反对它”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>115</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。他看到个人崇拜的结果是：“一般党员当然要以支书的意见为意见，所谓‘正义’‘公理’在这里是行不通的。在这里还要说‘群众意见’这是骗人”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>318</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">首长们享有特权，老百姓们自然有看法：“看见我们同志把很多吃不了的饭全喂了鸡，他们心疼死了，他们说‘我们在家里落下一粒饭也要拾起吃了啊！这里却喂鸡！”（上卷页</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>538</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）萧军有一段时间自愿离开延安到二十几里路外的农村生活，深切感受到那里的居民只有对公家尽义务的负担，没有权利的享受，更谈不上文化、卫生、教育等。一天晚饭后他到借住的村长家，村长小儿子问他：“你又来要什么来了啊？”萧军被这孩子的话震动了：“这说明‘公家人’到百姓家中是‘索要’，没有给与——这给与了孩子这印象”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>277</SPAN>）</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">。此后他“很不敢问一些人们的生活情形，因为他们总是诉说出公粮的数目多，而我又是个吃公粮的人！”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>288</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">萧军看到延安周边地区居民文化生活状况之差，出乎想象。一位刘姓老人谈起光绪年间的事：“那时候有主子，有地的纳粮，无地的纳税，官兵全有饷，馍只有六个麻钱一个……今刻……”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>317</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）这位老人留恋着自己的时代，左肩上还垂留着一条小辫子。萧军明白他对目前社会的不满和不解。他感觉到，“这里的人民对于共产党和政府是没有思想、感情上的连结的，他们只感到‘谁坐皇帝给谁纳税’的义务”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>293</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。对于中共当时力捧的劳动模范吴满有，当地老百姓也不买账，他们说，“他并不劳动啊……公家却还给他一头牛，一头马，还有种地的刈……”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>339</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）如此可反映边区人民的政治文化观念如秧歌词中所唱：“革命工作做得好，毛主席请你把官封……”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>331</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>延安的不平等还表现在女性地位方面。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">萧军初到延安就非常看不惯毛泽东和王明两位夫人的作风。他写道，延安的一些高级干部的女人，一嫁了好丈夫，就丧掉了自己的人格和工作岗位，随丈夫“光荣”去了。萧军把她们比作“月亮”，自身没有热力，借重丈夫的光辉，“蓝苹，孟庆树，就是此流人物的代表”，他甚至毫无保留地写下，“这些卑贱的东西们比外面社会以生殖器做工具而换得舒服的女人们还要自娇自傲，是没有什么两样的”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>8</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。在萧军看来，延安的一些女人很有一点虚荣、意志薄弱甚至情操低下，男人们则利用自己的地位等优越条件、利用女人还不够强健的弱点占有女人。他很打算写一些小说暴露这类“革命的流氓”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>74</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）！</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">还有一种萧军看不惯的现象是外国专家在延安的表现。有朋友告诉萧军“中央医院一个苏联外科大夫要找一个临时爱人的事，他是有老婆在本国的”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>68</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。萧军也曾亲见中央医院一位苏联外科医生把一只老百姓的狗随便用手枪射杀了，翻译还大叫让路人拖去吃狗肉；几天后又见这位医生骑马追着几个女孩子说笑，有点流氓性（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>106</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">延安的法制状况也远不能让萧军满意。他在一次参议会上听主席高岗报告，一位名叫李景林的参议员，因为枪支走火打死了人，主席团决定开除他的议员名额。这时萧军提出反对。他认为，根据“外面”的刑法总则，这个议员的罪名尚未确定，因此不能先行“褫夺公权”。他主张先停止其议员职务，交由检察机关调查；待审判庭判明罪行，再来决定处分。这件事让萧军对主席、政府和法律教授生出一种无知的可怜（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>333</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。萧军还听过延安财政厅长报告边区的经济状况，包括制作鸦片烟膏，他很难过。厅长说，“为了钱，除开我个人没有去抢人以外，几乎什么方法全做过了！”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>399</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）这让萧军不禁感慨：革命的花是从最卑污的粪壤里开出来的。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>萧军之所以不满这些现象，是因为他从心底里认为，这些不良现象如果发生在“外面”，那是没有什么的；但这是在延安，就分外令他惋惜和怜悯。他在日记中记下这些不满纯粹是从共产党利益出发，希望帮助共产党人建立德性，改变各种官僚主义习气和不良作风。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 16pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">萧军眼中的“整风”</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>从历史研究角度说，“整风”和“抢救”运动一起，都是延安“整风运动”的一部分</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="POSITION: relative; LETTER-SPACING: -0.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; TOP: -3pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-text-raise: 3.0pt">注</SPAN><SPAN style="POSITION: relative; LETTER-SPACING: -0.5pt; FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; TOP: -3pt; mso-text-raise: 3.0pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>。萧军作为一个旁观者和参与者，比较深入地观察了中下层干部整风情况和“抢救”运动状况，《日记》中这两部分内容着墨较多，这里分开来谈。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1942</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月毛泽东在中央党校发表讲话，整风运动正式开始。萧军对于整风运动在落实中的表现，总的讲是持批评态度的。《日记》记载：</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月某天，萧军“在中央研究院看他们‘整顿三风’的壁报”，看到“有些人被骂了：罗迈</SPAN></FONT><SPAN style="POSITION: relative; LETTER-SPACING: -0.5pt; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; TOP: -3pt; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-text-raise: 3.0pt">注</SPAN><SPAN style="POSITION: relative; LETTER-SPACING: -0.5pt; FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; TOP: -3pt; mso-text-raise: 3.0pt" lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">，欧阳山等，但那些文章却是粗糙的”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>426</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，萧军参加了题为《批评与自我批评》的整顿三风讨论会。他的观感是：“他们全是在那里瞪着眼睛扯谎”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>486</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>萧军对于整风运动的看法，从他对王实味事件的态度足可呈现。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>萧军和王实味并无太多交往，在他眼中，王实味不过是一个摇摇晃晃高个子，白面孔，半疯狂的神经质的人。后来王实味找萧军时，他还有避嫌之意，不愿理会。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">虽然中共对王实味的批判已经开始，但萧军在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1942</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>23</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日杨家岭召开的第三次文艺座谈会上，直陈自己对于王实味的观点：“我仍然承认他主观上是站在革命立场上”，“对于王实味批评的态度是不对的”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>476</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。某天他听说王实味自动脱党，他认为，如果有此说，“这是党内一些无知的人以批评为打击的结果”，“这是不能作为叛徒看的……只能说是革命逃兵”。他甚至专门为此去见毛泽东，毛“似乎很困疲和懊恼的样子，也许在烦厌我又去多事”，但萧军严正地陈述自己的意见，耐心解释，他觉得毛“似乎也明白了”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>488</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">日，萧军参加中央研究院第二次对王实味斗争会。开头两个发言的就从思想、政治、组织上用各种证据证明王实味是“托派”，有计划、有阴谋地破坏党、侮辱党。当王实味站起来辩解时，立刻就有人打断他的话。萧军感觉他们“像一群恶狗似的，伸出嘴巴向他围攻……而主席还纵容着不管，这简直是一种阴谋！”这场会议给萧军以强烈刺激，《日记》中不少笔墨写下了他的愤怒和痛恨，以至于他说“这是最不愉快的一天”，“我更确定我和这党的关系”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>490</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。几天后他见到王震，王震说王实味“简直不够一个文化人”，萧军继续解释说：这是对思想战斗问题，不是对人的问题（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>493</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">萧军对王实味的仗义执言遭到中央研究院等八大团体的反对，他竟然收到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>108</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">人签名抗议书，信中写道：“我们这次座谈会是清算王实味的托派思想及其反党活动……因此不仅党员应该积极拥护，而且一切党外人士也应抱同情的态度，给与积极的帮助”云云（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>501</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。有朋友善意提醒萧军明哲保身，“不要弄成王实味第二”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>595</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">但是在日后有机会的场合，萧军照样毫不妥协，坚定认为王实味不是托派，尤其反对“诬良为盗”的斗争办法（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>86</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。直到</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face=Calibri>1944</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">年，萧军检点旧刊物，细心重读王实味发表在《中国文化》第六期上的文章《文艺民族形式问题上的旧错误与新偏向》，他说“实在还寻不出他的‘托派’和‘特务’主张在哪里”，如果一定要说王实味是“反革命”，那么“只是这作者对于共产党两位愚蠢、武断、机械论者的‘理论家’——陈伯达，艾思奇——做了批评”，而且萧军认为这批评是应当的。因此萧军认为，政治上需要这样一个“牺牲者”，这是一种可怜的“政治戏法”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">下卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>346</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">总的来讲，萧军对中共提出的“批评与自我批评”十分不以为然，他觉得“他们口头上说不怕批评，不怕舆论，实际是外强中干，比方‘轻骑队’现在就不敢出来了，报上也再见不到一篇批评性的文章”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>527</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">整风运动的结果在延安以外的地方上更不能让萧军满意。年轻的作家柳青告诉萧军：“第一个感到负担重……地主与贫农苦，中农好……下级干部贪污腐化……军队的纪律也不好了……人民逃避兵役，并不如报上所说‘母送儿子去当兵’……这是仅有的偶然现象……人民对两个政权全是冷漠的，对于共产党本希望能好些，如果和国民党一样，他们就失望了……对法律不满……他们不乐意被选举，选举上是认为倒霉的事……人民不信任边币……”（</SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312">上卷页<SPAN lang=EN-US>600</SPAN></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">）。</SPAN></FONT></P>
