<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin">（一）</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>河南省兰考县县委书记焦裕禄的名字，对很多人来说并不陌生，上世纪</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>60</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年代，新华社一篇关于他的大通讯，使他轰动全国。而和他一起在兰考创建了一片新天地的县长张钦礼却鲜为人知。他俩是“亲密的战友”，却有着截然不同的两种命运。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>我提笔写这桩案件时，突然改变写法，把结尾移到开头。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼铁窗生涯</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>13</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年，出狱后于</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>2004</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>7</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日去世。兰考十万群众自发地为他举行了一个惊天地、泣鬼神的“囚徒荣归”的葬礼。兰考县城万人空巷，通往墓地的大小公路两旁、农家田埂地头，群众为他摆的祭品是一碗碗清水，是杂面窝窝头，赞扬他为官一生，两袖清风；怀念他在贫困饥饿的上世纪</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>60</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年代，和焦裕禄结伴带领他们走出困境、生出希望，使他们免受饥寒，甚至于饿死。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>我和老伴周原当日给他的唁电：“苍天有眼欲落泪，万民有口皆是碑。”</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>时隔九年，</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>2013</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月，张钦礼</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>88</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>岁的老伴刘秀芝因脑梗住院，想见我一面，</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>85</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>岁的我，冒着近</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>40</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>度的酷热，从北京到兰考，与秀芝数次相拥而泣后，即去拜祭张钦礼。他的墓地周围已成一片碑林。这些石碑、玉碑的立碑者，不仅来自兰考和河南其他县市，也有来自山东、河北、上海、台湾的。数不清多少万人在</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>60</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>多块墓碑、感恩碑上刻上自己的名字，好像每一块石碑、每一个人名，都有一段感人的故事。有一块台湾的感恩碑，特别耀眼，碑文记载：一位叫刘中豫的老兵，</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1949</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年跟随国民党去台湾时，撇下他</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>岁的孤儿，这个孤儿在张钦礼的关爱下长大成人，娶妻生子，就职于政府机关。碑文最后两句是：“以旌张公之德，彰显千秋大义。”那个年代的人都知道，谁要是扯上和台湾国民党有什么瓜葛，是要整肃的。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼三次遭难时，周恩来总理三次解救他，也没能改变他的命运。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>文革中，河南省委曾给中央一份报告，提出保护兰考、林县两个典型。毛主席在此报告上批示：“同意”。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>这是一个很复杂的历史过程，一个很复杂的历史现象。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>习近平担任副主席期间，在兰考视察时曾问过张钦礼的情况。时间是</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>2009</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日。他在一次座谈会上问：“当年和焦裕禄一起带领干部、群众除三害的那位县委领导是谁？”时任新闻干事的刘俊生答：“县委副书记张钦礼。”习又问，“这位同志还在吗？”刘答：“他去世了。”</FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年入党，</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1954</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年任兰考县县长，对兰考的历史和现状了如指掌，对兰考的群众有很深的感情。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>兰考座落在黄河边上，黄河多次在这里决口，形成了风沙、盐碱、内涝的自然灾害（</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 仿宋_GB2312"><FONT size=3>以下称三害</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>）。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年以前的兰考，经过治理，三害有很大的变化，虽说还是豫东老灾区的穷县之一，但群众的日子还过得去。大跃进反而使兰考更穷了。但当时的兰考主要不是自然灾害而是人祸。具体情况我后面再谈。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼的全部不幸在于他说真话。他为此付出了惨重的代价。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1957</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年反右时，兰考九百多名脱产干部，却划了</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>366</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>名右派。起因是县委组织部部长孙跃堂霸占了一名现役军人的未婚妻，和原来的妻子离了婚。不久那位军人复员回来，孙跃堂怕他“惹是生非”，捏造罪名将他打入监狱。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1957</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年反右时，开始叫帮助党整风，几个知情者在县委门口贴了一张大字报，揭露了这件事情的真相，随即就有二百多名干部在这张大字报上签名。后来转入反右时，这些签名者都划成右派。张钦礼为他们大声疾呼，鸣冤叫屈。为此，他受到了“撤销县委副书记的职务，内部控制使用”的处分。孙跃堂呢？虽有“错误”，但因“引蛇出洞”有功，原职原薪调到邻近的东明县工作，不久又晋升为县委副书记。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼的灾难由此开始。他作为党内“异端”的形象，像影子一样跟随着他直到生命的终结。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1958</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年秋，省里召开地、县秋粮征购大会，要求各县报产量、报征购数，报了三天，层层加码，也报不出省里要求的征购数字。张钦礼在会上发言了，他说他不仅报不出来，其他地县报出的数字都是假的。他批评省委带头搞浮夸，并指名道姓批评省委第一书记吴芝圃。张钦礼被押送兰考接受群众的批斗，批斗八个月，戴上了“右倾机会主义分子”的帽子，撤销党内外一切职务，留党查看一年，降了三级，送农村劳动改造。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼劳改的地方叫老君营，县里只给他</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>元的工资，每月还要交</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>元给生产队。开始在大食堂吃饭，</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1960</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年春群众普遍浮肿，村口路边经常有饿死的人。大食堂解散，张钦礼到群众家里吃派饭，有时吃的就是群众偷偷从外地讨来的发馊发霉的“百家饭”。当时外出逃荒被称为“盲流”是要受惩罚的，但这些去外地讨饭的群众不避讳他，告诉他不光兰考有饿死的人，外地也有，他们听说，信阳地区有的村庄人都饿死光了。有一段时间，张钦礼也饿得不省人事，妻子来看他，他不认识她，她千方百计把他救过来。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼开始给周总理写信。大意是：我受委屈事小，为了党和人民的利益，我不怕再遭横祸。他说“河南省委第一书记吴芝圃等省委领导压制民主，堵塞言路，不让人讲话。许多抵制浮夸风、共产风的好干部好党员横遭批斗，开除党籍；好多群众逃荒要饭，有的饿死。”这使他这个当县长的彻夜难眠。他指责“河南省委犯了掠夺农民的错误”，呼求总理快来救救河南人民。写完信的时间是</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1960</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>28</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>一天夜里，一群饥民来到张钦礼的草屋，叫他信不要寄出，免得被当地截扣后败露坏事，干脆去北京找周总理。他们为他准备路上吃的干粮，偷偷把他送到火车站，千叮咛，万嘱托，叫他到北京见不到周总理，打不赢这场官司就别回来。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼能见到周总理十分不易，门槛多，好同志也多，细写起来要用很多文字。总之，中南海接待处的一位马局长，先把他的信交给了周总理，安排好他的食宿叫他等待。</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1960</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>月</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>日上午</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>时，马局长带他去见周总理。周总理先看到张钦礼的信，见到他的第一句话就说：“你是全国两千多个县中，第一个向我反映真实情况的县长。”看着衣服破旧，面黄浮肿的张钦礼，总理叫工作人员先带他去吃饭，张钦礼说：“总理，不用麻烦了，我来时乡亲们给我带的干粮还没有吃完，我知道你很忙，现在只想和你多说说话。”总理叫他拿出干粮，看看群众都吃些什么，张钦礼开始有些犹豫，稍后慢慢从一个布袋中掏出一个菜团子，总理说：“给我尝尝！”伸手接时菜团子成了碎渣，掉到地上，这是用树叶掺一点杂粮做成的。总理弯腰拾一块放到嘴里，大声说：“我这个总理没当好。”总理眼里有泪光，张钦礼在一旁也哭了。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼从北京回来，关于他的平反，一波三折，拖了半年多。这中间地、县委给他平反留个尾巴，他不签字，又回到他劳改的老君营，和群众一起劳动，一起过着半饥半饱的生活。直到有一天，新上任的省委第一书记刘建勋派人来找到他。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>他先在省委大院碰到已经停职反省的吴芝圃，吴问他：“听说你去中央反映河南的问题？”张答：“我去了，连你也告了。”吴芝圃一时语塞，又问：“你爹可好？”张答：“我爹不好，饿得浮肿。”说着，用手指按了自己的腮帮，按了个坑，说：“我也浮肿。”这样的一问一答显然很不愉快，张钦礼赶紧走了。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>刘建勋临危受命，来河南解决饥饿的问题。他来河南前，请示周总理，总理给他两个字“化缘”。刘建勋理解是“要饭”，就是向一些稍微富裕的省、市“要粮、要菜、要一切可能吃的东西”。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>张钦礼见到刘建勋，刘对他说，国务院来了电话，问你的情况，让我听听你的意见。张钦礼谈了群众的饥饿和他的平反阻力。刘建勋请他吃了饭，代表省委正式给他平反。他回到兰考恢复了县委副书记和县长的职务，领取了三年补发的</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1246</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>元工资，把它全部捐给了“县救灾办公室”。这笔钱对张钦礼一家可不是小数目，他的夫人月工资</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>40</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>多元，要养活五个孩子和她自己。张钦礼对妻子说，群众比我们更穷，有的正在挨饿。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>刘建勋了解了像张钦礼这样的干部，平反的阻力这么大。随即以省委的名义下达文件：凡在反对浮夸风中受处罚的同志，他们的问题一风吹。</FONT></SPAN></P>

<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>焦裕禄</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>1962</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>年冬由洛阳矿山机械厂调到兰考任县委书记，他在这个工厂工作十年。他带着大企业熏陶出的新知识、新思维来到兰考。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>不错，他是带着中央八届十中全会、毛主席重提阶级斗争的精神来到兰考。当时兰考的情况怎么样呢？全年粮食的总产量只有六千万斤，是大跃进前的三分之一；以粮为纲，占全年总收入三分之一的芝麻、花生、西瓜等经济作物，有的连种子都没有了；大批群众外出逃荒要饭；大批牲畜死亡；树立在大风口上的五十华里长、十道防护林带近两百万棵树，大办钢铁时，全部砍光，风沙再起，形成新的沙丘群；一个总人口</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>36</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>万的县，三年来人口减少了</FONT></SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT size=3 face=Calibri>12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>万，不能说这些都是饿死的，一是饿死的，二是农村妇女不生育，还有其他原因。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>县委大院有一个办公室，叫“劝阻办公室”，从全城各个出口处劝说堵截外出讨饭的饥民，这叫阶级斗争，因为他们出去讨饭给“社会主义脸上抹黑”。留在家里的群众，坐等救济，又是一大批干部把国家的救济粮分送到群众手里。在没有任何副食的情况下，每人每天六两原粮。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>焦裕禄撤下“劝阻办公室”的牌子，换上“除三害办公室”的牌子，调公社干部卓兴隆任“除三害办公室”主任。使领导救灾的干部成为除三害的干部，坐等救济的群众，成为生产自救的群众。坐等救济是伸手要恩赐；生产自救，是创造自己的生活。焦裕禄还到火车站，对外流讨饭的群众不劝阻不堵截，而是把他们送上火车，并嘱托他们，讨饭的地点不要太分散，便于大家互相照应。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-latin; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-latin"><FONT size=3>他树立了四种不同类型的生产自救的典型，称他们为“四杆旗”。它们是秦寨、赵垛楼、双杨树、韩村。</FONT></SPAN></P>
