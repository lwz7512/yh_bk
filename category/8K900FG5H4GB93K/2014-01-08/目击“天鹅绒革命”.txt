<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt; mso-font-kerning: 0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: FZHTJW--GB1-0" lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 黑体; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3>&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我在离开捷克斯洛伐克</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个月之后，受新华社总社的派遣于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1988</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月底重返布拉格分社任首席记者。我这次赴任经历了捷克斯洛伐克的巨变。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">纪念帕拉赫掀起风暴</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我重返布拉格后不久，便目睹了这里风云激荡的一周。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日是布拉格查理大学哲学系学生扬·帕拉赫为反抗苏联入侵自焚</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">周年。帕拉赫目睹祖国的主权惨遭苏联的践踏，当局又无力对抗，满腔悲愤。这位在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1969</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日只有</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">岁的大学生，便采取了自焚的行动，表示对苏联侵略的抗议。广大人民群众深为这位青年的爱国行动所震撼，在民族蒙受外强欺压的情况下，帕拉赫成了人民心目中的英雄，每年这一天都举行悼念他的活动，表达对苏联占领的不满和抗议。早在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日之前，一个署名“集体自杀组织委员会——火炬</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">号”的青年人，给不少组织写信，表示要在这一天为纪念帕拉赫而自焚。连日来，布拉格气氛紧张。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">15</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日达到高峰。为了报道实情，我上午</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">点多钟就来到瓦茨拉夫广场观察情况。这时，在整个广场和与其垂直的普什科普大街上，布满武装警察。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">点后，有上万人涌到这里，悼念这位年轻的爱国志士。捷通讯社和电视台在当日的新闻广播中称这次集会“是西方颠覆中心和某些西方电台伙同‘七七宪章’运动策划的挑衅，目的是煽起反社会主义情绪。为了维护首都的安宁，公安部队逮捕了</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">91</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">人。”</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">警察对群众动武的消息，顷刻传遍全城。从</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日起，数以万计的群众又到瓦茨拉夫广场悼念帕拉赫，群众示威一直持续了</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">周。示威者除呼喊“自由”、“人权”，“要戈尔巴乔夫和杜布切克”的口号外，还呼喊打倒捷共中央总书记雅克什的口号。示威人数之多，持续时间之长，为二十多年来所没有。当局逮捕了包括著名作家、“七七宪章”运动领导人哈韦尔等上百人，对他们进行公开审讯，以“煽动罪”判处哈韦尔</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个月的徒刑。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><FONT size=3>对哈韦尔等的审讯并没有平息事态，相反地在国内外引起更大的抗议浪潮。捷克斯洛伐克文艺界、科技界和宗教界人士上书联邦总理阿达麦茨，谴责当局的镇压行动，要求释放哈韦尔等被捕者，并希望同当局就社会问题进行对话。</FONT></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">在一月风暴之后，捷克斯洛伐克国内的形势更加动荡，反对派与当局之间的斗争更加激烈。当局动员一切力量谴责示威活动。捷共中央主席团、民族阵线中央主席团、工会中央理事会均明确表示支持保安部队的镇压行径，指责示威的组织者为反国家分子，得到西方的支持等。雅克什等捷共领导人，死抱着关于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年的“布拉格之春”是反革命的结论不放，他们意识到，在这个问题上的任何松动，他们的政治生涯就要完结。他们把维护自己的地位和维护捷共的领导作用及社会主义制度等同起来，因此广大群众更把他们和捷共视为一体，把攻击的矛头对准捷共。在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月底举行的中央全会上，雅克什表示，决不能向反对派和教会势力让步，不能改变在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年问题上的立场。捷共党报《红色权利报》在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月底发表长文，批判</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年“布拉格之春”的领导人杜布切克，指责他“完全和有意识地站到了修正主义立场上，积极实施修正主义纲领，把国家引入严重的危机之中”，重申</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年苏联等</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">国对捷克斯洛伐克的入侵是为了“制止反革命”，是“国际主义援助”。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">引人注目的是，在一片对示威的谴责声中联邦政府的态度却不同。与</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年事件牵连较少的阿达麦茨总理</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">16-18</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日赴苏联访问。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">17</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日受到苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫的接见。戈尔巴乔夫向他详细了解了捷国内的情况，并向他介绍了苏联的改革经验。这是近</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个月来他同戈尔巴乔夫的第二次会见。他在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">18</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日结束对苏联的访问时向捷克斯洛伐克记者发表谈话说，“必须习惯某些我们过去不习惯的新做法。我们愿同一切真诚地、想以创造性方式帮助社会主义的人进行对话。”阿达麦茨回国后在斯洛伐克同青年会见，坦率交谈。他在会见中谈到了捷克斯洛伐克人民关切的两大问题：一是对历史的评价，也就是对</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年“布拉格之春”的评价问题；二是对话问题。他强调指出，“评价历史不容拖延，因为社会需要客观真实的看法”。他还说，“对历史问题要从个人的立场超脱出来是不容易的，但必须这样做，对此应该理解。如果昨日的经验不打开我们的眼界，我们就不能摆脱过时看法的束缚”。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; TEXT-INDENT: 35.15pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal align=center><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; FONT-SIZE: 14pt; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">《几句话》宣言吹响号角</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt" lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p><FONT size=3 face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">在一月风潮之后，各种组织纷纷建立。特别引人注目的是捷共</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年时期的一些领导人重整旗鼓，于</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月份建立了“社会主义复兴俱乐部”，表示拥护</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">4</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月《行动纲领》中所提出的杜布切克的政策，拥护戈尔巴乔夫的改革主张，并愿同一切改革力量联合。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; <P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">20</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，即在苏联侵略捷克斯洛伐克</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">周年前夕，布拉格市中心的瓦茨拉夫广场上，笼罩着一片肃穆紧张的气氛。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">上午</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">点钟，我到达了广场，看到所有通往广场的道路皆被武装警察封锁。虽然行人可以穿行，但车辆，除公车外，一律不得通行。在这个长</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">680</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">多米，宽近</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">61</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">米的广场上，没有昔日熙熙攘攘的人群，手持报话机，腰系手枪和警棍的警察以及一些便衣特工，格外引人注目。广场两侧的建筑物和电线杆上已经架好了摄像机，穿梭般的警车，疾驰而过。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，即苏联入侵捷克斯洛伐克</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">周年纪念日，布拉格市的群众举行了声势浩大的示威，要求宣布苏联入侵非法和为“布拉格之春”平反。</SPAN></FONT></P>
<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">从下午</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">3</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">时起，数以千计的人们，从四面八方向瓦茨拉夫广场汇集，其中多数为年轻人。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">5</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">时许，广场上的人群肃立，为反抗苏联入侵而牺牲的人们默哀。</SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">全副武装的警察包围了整个广场，他们用高音喇叭宣布示威非法，号召人们散开。示威群众没有听从，这时警察便排成人墙，把人群挤出广场和与广场垂直的步行街。晚上</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">时许，被驱散的示威群众，又汇拢起来，警察又调集了大批警车，重新包围了广场，并用警棍再次驱散了示威群众。</SPAN></FONT></P>

<P style="TEXT-INDENT: 26.25pt; MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.5" class=MsoNormal><FONT size=3><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">这次示威和</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">21</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日的示威相比，规模更大，呼喊的口号更为激烈，矛头直指</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US><FONT face="Times New Roman">1968</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年苏联侵捷后被扶持上台的捷共领导人。它预示着更大的风暴将会来临。</SPAN></FONT></P>
