<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; TEXT-ALIGN: center" align=center><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 7.5pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 黑体">慈禧与光绪<SPAN lang=EN-US><o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1908</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月清朝宣布预备立宪以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年为限，在中国历史上第一次提出了宪政时间表。在此前后，清廷为预备立宪做了大量工作，中国社会呈现民主社会的雏形。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">一</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　宪政也称“宪政民主”、“立宪政体”，是指以宪法为前提、以民主为核心、以保障人权为目的的政治形态或政治过程。宪政的本质是限制政府权力和保障公民的权利，其作用是防止政府滥用权力，维护公民的自由和权利。宪法与宪政有别，宪法是文本、宣言；宪政是实践、制度。有宪法未必有宪政，有宪政必然有宪法。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　清朝最后</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年，是中国由专制向民主转型的关键时期。面对外力的压迫和国内要求变革的形势，清朝在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1901</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">—</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1911</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年对社会各方面进行了一系列改革，这些改革被称为清末新政。在清末新政中，最重要的是日俄战争后的政治改革，即</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1905</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">—</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1911</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年的预备立宪。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1904</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">—</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1905</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年在中国领土上爆发了日俄战争，结果君主立宪制的蕞尔小国日本战胜了农奴制的庞然大国俄罗斯。国内外舆论普遍认为：日胜俄败，是立宪战胜专制的铁证。战争初期西方报纸就曾指出：“此战非俄日之战也，乃立宪、专制两治术之战也。”国内报纸也强调，这场战争“非日俄之战，而立宪，专制二政体之战也”；“非小国能战胜于大国，实立宪能战胜于专制”；“日本以立宪而胜，俄国以专制而败”。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　在这一背景下，实行宪政、加快进行政治体制改革，成为许多高级官员和社会贤达的强烈呼声。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　日俄战争一爆发，</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1904</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">2</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月云贵总督丁振铎、云南巡抚林绍年便联电奏请变法。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1905</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">6</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月，袁世凯联合两江总督周馥和湖广总督张之洞电奏，请以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">12</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年为期实行立宪。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1907</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">10</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月，著名学者马相伯在政闻社成立大会上大声疾呼废除专制，他在《政党之必要及其责任》的演说中指出：“天下虽无绝对之良政治，而有绝对的恶政治……质而言之，则曰专制。专制政治，束缚人人之神我，使不得申，故有国家曾不如其无。故生为专制之国民者，必当以排除专制为唯一之义务。”</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　这些高级官员和社会贤达在立宪问题上的共识和呼吁，成为清末预备立宪的重要动力。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　在朝野上下一致要求立宪的形势下，慈禧太后为首的清王朝顺应时代潮流，开始了史无前例的宪政改革。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p><FONT face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</FONT></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">二</SPAN></B><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></B></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1908</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">8</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月，清朝宣布了宪政时间表：预备立宪以</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1909</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">进行省咨议局选举；</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1910</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年资政院开院；</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1917</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年召开国会，实行宪政</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; <P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　早在</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1906</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">9</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">月</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">1</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">日，清朝就发布了《宣示预备立宪谕》。谕旨指出：“各国之所以富强者，实由于实行宪法，取决公论，君民一体，呼吸相通，博采众长，明定权限，以及筹备财政，经画政务，无不公之于黎庶……时处今日，惟有及时详晰甄核，仿行宪政，大权统于朝廷，庶政公诸舆论，以立国家万年有道之基。”鉴于规制未备、民智未开等，“俟数年后，规模粗具，查看情形，参用各国成法，妥议立宪实行期限，再行宣布天下”（《近代中国宪政历程：史料汇萃》，中国政法大学出版社</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">2004</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">年版，第</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><FONT face="Times New Roman">52</FONT></SPAN><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">页）。虽然仍强调“大权统于朝廷，庶政公诸舆论”，但不可否认，该谕旨是中国专制统治者第一次对宪政制度的肯定和对两千年专制制度的否定。</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><o:p></o:p></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%"><SPAN style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 150%; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">　　两年后，清朝宣布了</SPAN><SPAN lang=EN-US style="FONT-SIZE: 9pt;
